## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will CBD show up on drug tests if I'm a professional streamer?


> For more information, see our guide on [CBD for Veterans: What Former Service Members Should Know](/articles/cbd-for-veterans).
Most CBD products contain trace amounts of THC (under 0.3%), which could potentially accumulate and show up on sensitive drug tests with heavy use. If you're concerned about sponsorship or employment screening, consider broad-spectrum or isolate CBD products that contain zero THC, though they may be less effective than full-spectrum options.

### Can I take CBD while drinking energy drinks or [coffee](/conditions/coffee) during streams?

CBD can be safely combined with caffeine, and many streamers find this combination helpful for maintaining alertness while staying calm. Start with lower CBD doses (10-15mg) when combining with stimulants, as CBD may either enhance or counteract caffeine's effects depending on your individual response and timing.

### What's the best CBD product type for quick relief during technical difficulties?

Sublingual tinctures or vape products provide the fastest relief (5-15 minutes) for sudden streaming stress like technical crashes or difficult chat situations. Keep a tincture bottle nearby for quick under-the-tongue dosing, or consider fast-acting gummies if you prefer not to use liquid products on camera.

### How long should I wait between CBD doses during marathon streaming sessions?

Wait at least 2-4 hours between doses during extended streams to avoid oversedation and maintain consistent effects. For 8+ hour streams, consider taking your initial dose 30 minutes before going live, then a smaller maintenance dose at the 4-6 hour mark if needed.

### Does CBD affect my reaction time for competitive gaming streams?

CBD typically doesn't impair reaction time at moderate doses (15-25mg), but higher amounts may cause mild sedation that could affect competitive performance. Test your response during practice sessions first, and consider using lower doses or timing intake for after competitive segments if you notice any impact on gameplay.
