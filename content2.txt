## Quick Summary

Bruxism—unconscious teeth grinding and jaw clenching—can damage teeth and cause pain. CBD's muscle-relaxing and stress-reducing properties may help decrease grinding intensity, particularly for stress-related bruxism.

## Understanding Bruxism

### What Is Bruxism?

Characteristics:
- Teeth grinding (often at night)
- Jaw clenching
- Often unconscious
- Can be severe
- Causes multiple problems

### Types of Bruxism

Categories:
- Sleep bruxism (nighttime)
- Awake bruxism (daytime)
- Primary (no clear cause)
- Secondary (related to other factors)

### Consequences

If untreated:
- Tooth damage
- Jaw pain (TMJ)
- Headaches
- Worn enamel
- Sleep disruption

## My Take

Bruxism is often closely tied to stress and anxiety, which is where CBD shows real potential. By addressing the stress component, CBD may help reduce the intensity and frequency of grinding, even if it doesn't stop it completely.

But don't skip the night guard because you're taking CBD. Teeth damage is permanent, and even reduced grinding can cause problems over time. Think of CBD as helping with the cause (stress, muscle tension) while the night guard protects from the consequences (tooth damage).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What time should I take CBD oil for nighttime teeth grinding?

Take [CBD oil](/glossary/cbd-oil) 30-60 minutes before bedtime to allow absorption and muscle relaxation effects to begin. For sleep bruxism specifically, timing is crucial since you want peak relaxation during your deepest sleep phases when grinding typically occurs most intensely.

### Can I use CBD gummies instead of oil for bruxism, or do I need the oil?

CBD gummies work for bruxism but take 1-2 hours to absorb, so take them earlier in the evening. Oil works faster (15-45 minutes) and may be more practical for bedtime routines. Both can provide the muscle relaxation and stress relief that may help reduce grinding intensity.

### How long does it typically take to notice less teeth grinding with CBD?

Most people report initial improvements in sleep quality and jaw tension within 3-7 days of consistent use. However, significant reduction in grinding intensity often takes 2-4 weeks as your stress levels and sleep patterns improve. Track your symptoms and any tooth damage progression with your dentist.

### Will CBD help with daytime jaw clenching or just nighttime grinding?

CBD can help with both daytime jaw clenching and nighttime grinding since both involve muscle tension and stress responses. For daytime clenching, smaller doses (5-10mg) taken during the day may help maintain relaxation without causing drowsiness, while evening doses target sleep bruxism.

### What CBD dosage range works best for teeth grinding and jaw tension?

Start with 10-15mg about an hour before bed and adjust weekly based on results. Many people find effective relief in the 15-25mg range for nighttime bruxism. If stress-related daytime clenching is also an issue, consider splitting the dose with 5-10mg during the day and 10-20mg before bed.
