# CBD for Burnout: Managing Exhaustion and Stress

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Burnout is a state of chronic stress leading to physical and emotional exhaustion. CBD's stress-reducing and sleep-supporting properties may help manage burnout symptoms while you address underlying causes.

---

## Understanding Burnout

### What It Is

Characteristics:
- Chronic workplace/life stress
- Physical and emotional exhaustion
- Cynicism and detachment
- Reduced effectiveness
- Feeling overwhelmed

### Signs of Burnout

Symptoms:
- Persistent fatigue
- Sleep problems
- Difficulty concentrating
- Irritability
- Physical symptoms (headaches, illness)
- Loss of motivation

---

## How CBD Might Help

### Stress Response

Benefits:
- CBD reduces cortisol (stress hormone)
- Calms nervous system
- Supports stress resilience
- Addresses physiological stress

### Sleep Support

Important for recovery:
- Burnout disrupts sleep
- Sleep is essential for recovery
- CBD supports sleep quality
- Rest enables healing

### Anxiety Reduction

Common with burnout:
- Anxiety about performance
- Overwhelming feelings
- CBD's anxiolytic effects
- Provides some relief

---

## What CBD Won't Do

### Limitations

Be realistic:
- Won't fix the root cause
- Doesn't change workload
- Not a substitute for boundaries
- Symptom support only

### Address the Cause

Essential:
- Identify burnout sources
- Set boundaries
- Reduce demands if possible
- Sometimes bigger changes needed

---

## Using CBD for Burnout

### Approach

Strategy:
- Daily use for consistent support
- Evening dose for sleep
- Moderate doses (20-40mg)
- Part of broader recovery plan

### Self-Care Foundation

CBD works best with:
- Adequate sleep priority
- Regular breaks
- Physical activity
- Social connection
- Professional help if needed

---

## When More Is Needed

### Professional Help

Consider if:
- Burnout is severe
- Unable to function
- Depressive symptoms
- Need workplace changes
- Medical evaluation warranted

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD may help** burnout symptoms
2. **Addresses stress** and sleep
3. **Won't fix** underlying causes
4. **Part of** broader recovery
5. **Seek help** for severe burnout

---
