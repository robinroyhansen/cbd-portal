## Why Post-Workout Matters

### Adaptation Happens During Recovery

Key concept:
- Training creates stimulus
- Recovery creates adaptation
- Poor recovery = poor results
- CBD supports recovery phase

### Recovery Challenges

Common issues:
- Excessive inflammation
- Poor sleep despite fatigue
- Muscle soreness (DOMS)
- Stress affecting recovery

---

## How CBD Supports Recovery

### Inflammation Management

After training:
- Exercise causes inflammation
- Some inflammation necessary
- Excessive inflammation problematic
- CBD modulates (doesn't eliminate)

### Sleep Quality

Critical for gains:
- Growth hormone during sleep
- Muscle repair during rest
- Training can disrupt sleep
- CBD supports quality rest

### Stress Response

Nervous system:
- Hard training is stress
- Chronic stress impairs recovery
- CBD helps shift to parasympathetic
- Better recovery state

## Post-Workout CBD Protocol

### Timing

When to take:
- Within 1-2 hours post-workout
- Evening dose before bed
- Both if possible
- Consistency matters

### Dosing

Typical approach:
- 25-40mg post-workout
- Higher after intense sessions
- Sleep-focused evening dose
- Adjust based on training load

### Delivery Methods

Options:
- Systemic (oil, capsule) for overall
- Topical for specific muscles
- Combine for best effect
- Post-workout shake inclusion

## What CBD Won't Do

### Realistic Expectations

CBD doesn't:
- Build muscle
- Burn fat
- Replace nutrition
- Substitute for sleep
- Eliminate all soreness

### Still Essential

You need:
- Proper training
- Adequate protein
- Sufficient calories
- Quality sleep
- Adequate rest days

## My Take

CBD fits well into a post-workout recovery routine, particularly for managing the inflammation and sleep disruption that can follow hard training. It won't replace proper nutrition, rest, or training, but it supports the recovery process that makes training effective.

The biggest benefit for many athletes is sleep support. Training hard then sleeping poorly is counterproductive. CBD helps you get the quality rest where adaptation actually happens.
