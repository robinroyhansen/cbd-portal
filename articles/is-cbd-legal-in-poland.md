# Is CBD Legal in Poland? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Poland** when derived from industrial hemp containing less than 0.3% THC. Poland has developed a growing CBD market, though it operates in a somewhat grey regulatory area. Products are widely available in specialized shops and online, with the market continuing to mature.

---

## Legal Framework

### Current Status

Poland's CBD regulations:
- Industrial hemp cultivation is legal
- THC limit: 0.3% (higher than some EU countries)
- CBD products are available commercially
- Novel Food regulations apply to edibles
- Market operates openly

### Hemp Cultivation

Poland is a significant hemp producer in Europe:
- Long agricultural tradition with hemp
- Industrial hemp cultivation legal
- Growing domestic CBD industry
- Hemp farming increasing

---

## What's Legal

### Clearly Legal

- Hemp-derived CBD with <0.3% THC
- CBD cosmetics and topicals
- Hemp food products (seeds, oil)
- CBD oils (marketed appropriately)

### Grey Area

- CBD as food supplements
- Novel Food considerations
- Some product categories unclear

### Prohibited

- THC above 0.3%
- Medical claims without authorization
- Products marketed as medicines
