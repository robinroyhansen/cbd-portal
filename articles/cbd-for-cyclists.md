# CBD for Cyclists: Endurance, Recovery, and Comfort

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Summary

Cyclists endure long hours in the saddle, accumulating fatigue, inflammation, and sleep debt. CBD has gained popularity in cycling for recovery support, sleep improvement, and managing the chronic aches of high-volume training.

---

## Why Cyclists Use CBD

### Cycling Demands

The sport requires:
- Hours of endurance
- High training volumes
- Recovery management
- Sleep despite fatigue
- Managing saddle soreness and aches

### CBD Applications

Benefits:
- Anti-inflammatory recovery
- Sleep support
- Pain management
- Non-impairing option

## WADA Status

### For Competitive Cyclists

Rules:
- CBD is permitted
- THC is prohibited
- Threshold exists for THC
- Use verified THC-free products

### UCI and Domestic Racing

Considerations:
- Same WADA rules
- THC-free essential if tested
- Third-party verification
- Document your products

## Recovery Protocol

### Post-Ride

Approach:
- CBD after long rides
- Topical on specific soreness
- Evening dose for sleep
- Consistent during training blocks

### Dosing

Suggestions:
- Daily: 25-50mg
- After hard rides: 30-50mg
- Sleep: 25-40mg
- Adjust for training load
