By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Answer

CBD exists in a **complex legal situation in Morocco**. Morocco legalized cannabis cultivation for medical and industrial purposes in 2021, but CBD consumer products are not yet widely regulated. Traditional hash production exists, but legal CBD products remain limited. Exercise caution.
