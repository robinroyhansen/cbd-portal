# Is CBD Legal in Laos? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Laos**. Cannabis and all derivatives are prohibited under Lao law. Despite casual presence in some areas, the legal prohibition is real and penalties can be severe.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Laos position:
- All cannabis prohibited
- CBD not distinguished
- Penalties include imprisonment
- Enforcement can be strict

### Reality vs Perception

Understanding:
- Cannabis visible in some contexts
- Doesn't mean it's legal
- Tourist areas may seem relaxed
- Law still applies

---

## For Visitors

### Warning

Critical:
- Do not bring CBD to Laos
- All cannabis derivatives illegal
- Penalties can be serious
- Don't rely on apparent tolerance

### At Borders

Important:
- Customs enforcement
- International entry points serious
- Don't risk it
- Plan without CBD
