- Walking down the aisle
- Saying vows without crying/blanking

## Wedding Day Protocol

### Morning Routine

Start the day:
- Light CBD dose with breakfast
- Allows time to settle
- Combine with calming activities
- Don't rush

### Pre-Ceremony Timing

Before the ceremony:
- Take 1-2 hours before
- Allow onset time
- Last chance before walking
- Moderate dose

### Throughout the Day

Considerations:
- Long day ahead
- May need additional doses
- Reception anxiety different
- Plan for the full event

## Dosing Recommendations

### Finding Right Amount

Balance:
- Enough to calm nerves
- Not so much you're drowsy
- Stay emotionally present
- Test beforehand

### Typical Approach

Suggestions:
- Morning: 10-15mg
- Pre-ceremony: 15-25mg
- Reception: as needed
- Adjust based on testing

- Verify timing works

### Practice Events

Good testing opportunities:
- Rehearsal dinner

## Alcohol Considerations

### CBD and Champagne

Important:
- CBD may intensify alcohol
- Wedding toasts involve drinking
- Be cautious with combination
- Know your response

### Strategy

Approach:
- Lower CBD if drinking
- Pace alcohol carefully
- Stay hydrated
- Don't overdo either

## My Take

Wedding day anxiety is so common that there's an entire industry around it. CBD offers a natural way to take the edge off without the numbness that some anti-anxiety options create. You want to feel present and emotionally available for your wedding, not sedated through it.

The key is testing your protocol beforehand. Your wedding is not the day to discover CBD makes you drowsy or doesn't work at your chosen dose. Test at similar events so you know exactly what to expect.
