# Is CBD Legal in Ghana? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Ghana**. Ghana's Narcotics Control Commission Act classifies cannabis and its derivatives as controlled substances. No distinction is made between CBD and THC under Ghanaian law.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Ghana's stance:
- Cannabis completely prohibited
- All derivatives controlled
- No CBD exceptions
- No medical cannabis program

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Imprisonment (5-10 years possible)
- Heavy fines
- Criminal record
- Deportation for foreigners

---

## For Travelers

### Essential Guidance

Do not:
- Bring any CBD products
- Assume THC-free is acceptable
- Rely on foreign prescriptions
- Take any risks

### Reality

Note:

- Not worth the risk

Ghana maintains traditional prohibition on all cannabis products. There's no legal distinction for CBD, and penalties can be serious. For any Ghana visit, plan to go without CBD products.
