# Is CBD Legal in Vietnam? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Vietnam**. All cannabis-derived products are prohibited under Vietnamese law. There are no exemptions for hemp-derived CBD. **Do not bring CBD to Vietnam.**

---

## Vietnam's Drug Laws

### Legal Framework

Vietnam's position:
- Cannabis prohibited entirely
- CBD included in prohibition
- No hemp exemption
- Strict enforcement

### Classification

CBD status:
- Treated as cannabis product
- No legal distinction
- Possession is criminal
- Zero tolerance

---

## Penalties

### Consequences

Vietnam penalties:
- Possession: Prison sentences
- Use: Fines and rehabilitation
- Trafficking: Severe penalties including death
- No CBD exception
