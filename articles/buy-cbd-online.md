# How to Buy CBD Online Safely

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Buying CBD online offers the best selection and often better prices, but requires careful vetting of sellers. Focus on verifying third-party testing, checking company legitimacy, reading reviews, and understanding return policies. Reputable online sellers provide more information than physical stores, making informed choices easier.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Online often offers better selection and value
- Verify seller legitimacy first
- Check for third-party testing
- Read return and shipping policies
- Use secure payment methods

---

## Advantages of Buying Online

### Why Online Shopping Works

**Better Selection:**
- More brands available
- Full product ranges
- Specialty products
- Compare easily

**Better Information:**
- Lab reports accessible
- Detailed descriptions
- Customer reviews
- Company background

**Better Value:**
- No retail markup
- Subscription discounts
- Sales and promotions
- Compare prices easily

---

## Step 1: Verify the Seller

### Essential Checks

**Company Legitimacy:**
- Physical address listed
- Contact information available
- Company registration details
- Clear "About Us" section

**Website Security:**
- HTTPS (padlock icon)
- Secure payment processing
- Privacy policy present
- Professional appearance

### Red Flags

**Avoid Sites With:**
- No company information
- No physical address
- Suspicious payment only
- Poor website quality
- No contact methods

---

## Step 2: Check Testing and Transparency

### Lab Reports (COAs)

**Should Be:**
- Easy to find on site
- Current (within 6-12 months)
- Batch-specific
- From independent lab

**Must Include:**
- Cannabinoid potency
- Contaminant testing
- Pass/fail clearly shown
- Lab name and date

### If No Lab Reports

Don't buy. Period. This is non-negotiable.

---

## Step 3: Research the Brand

### Background Check

**Look For:**
- Years in business
- Industry presence
- Media coverage
- Awards/certifications

**Review Sources:**
- Trustpilot
- Google Reviews
- Reddit mentions
- Industry publications

### Social Proof

- Active social media
- Customer engagement
- Community presence
- Responsive to questions

---

## Step 4: Evaluate Products

### Product Pages Should Include

**Essential Information:**
- Full ingredient list
- CBD content per serving
- Total CBD in product
- Spectrum type
- Extraction method
- Hemp source

**Nice to Have:**
- Terpene profiles
- Certificate downloads
- Usage instructions
- Storage information

---

## Step 5: Understand Policies

### Shipping

**Check:**
- Where they ship
- Shipping costs
- Delivery timeframes
- Packaging discretion
- Tracking provided

### Returns and Refunds

**Good Policies Include:**
- Clear return window (30+ days)
- Refund process explained
- Return shipping terms
- Satisfaction guarantees

### Customer Service

**Test Before Buying:**
- Send a question
- Note response time
- Evaluate helpfulness
- Check multiple contact options

---

## Payment Safety

### Secure Methods

**Safest:**
- Credit card (chargeback protection)
- PayPal (buyer protection)
- Established payment processors

**More Risky:**
- Direct bank transfer
- Cryptocurrency only
- Unusual payment methods

### Protect Yourself

- Don't save payment details
- Use secure networks
- Monitor statements
- Keep order confirmations

---

## First Purchase Tips

### Smart Approach

1. **Start small** — Don't bulk buy first time
2. **Single product** — Test before expanding
3. **Track everything** — Save confirmations
4. **Note arrival** — Check condition
5. **Test product** — Verify quality

### Document Your Order

- Order confirmation
- Tracking information
- Product batch number
- COA for your batch

## Where to Buy

### Best Options

**Brand Direct:**
- Best prices often
- Full selection
- Fresh products
- Direct support

**Established Marketplaces:**
- Multiple brands
- Easy comparison
- Customer reviews
- Buyer protection

### Avoid

- Random marketplace sellers
- Social media only sellers
- Unverified websites
- "Too good to be true" deals

---

## Subscription Considerations

### Benefits

- 15-20% discount typically
- Convenience
- Never run out
- Often free shipping

### Before Subscribing

- Test product first
- Understand cancellation
- Know frequency options
- Check price vs. one-time

---

## International Buying

### If Buying From Abroad

**Consider:**
- Import regulations
- Customs risk
- Shipping costs
- Return difficulty
- THC content differences

### Usually Better

Buy from your own country/region when possible.

---

## After Purchase

### When Package Arrives

**Verify:**
- Correct products
- Seal intact
- Batch matches COA
- Expiry date acceptable
- No damage

### If Issues

- Document immediately
- Contact seller quickly
- Keep all packaging
- Request resolution

---

## My Take

Online is how I buy CBD, and it's how most informed consumers buy. You have access to more information, better prices, and easier comparison than any physical store offers. The key is doing your homework: verify the company, check the testing, read reviews, and start with a small order. Once you find a brand you trust, online buying becomes the most convenient and cost-effective way to maintain your CBD supply.
