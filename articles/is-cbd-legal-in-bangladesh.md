# Is CBD Legal in Bangladesh? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Bangladesh**. The country prohibits all cannabis-derived products under the Narcotics Control Act. No distinction is made between CBD and THC. Do not bring CBD to Bangladesh.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Bangladesh's position:
- All cannabis prohibited
- CBD not exempted
- Narcotics Control Act enforcement
- Severe penalties

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Lengthy imprisonment possible
- Heavy fines
- Criminal charges
- Deportation for foreigners

---

## For Visitors

### Warning

Essential:
- Never bring CBD products
- All forms prohibited
- No medical exceptions
- Not worth any risk

Bangladesh maintains strict cannabis prohibition. There is no legal pathway for CBD use, and the risks are serious. Plan to go without CBD during any Bangladesh visit.
