# CBD and Immune Support: What We Know

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD's relationship with the immune system is complex. Research suggests CBD has immunomodulatory properties—neither simply boosting nor suppressing immunity. Understanding this nuance helps set realistic expectations about CBD and immune health.

---

## The Endocannabinoid System and Immunity

### ECS in Immune Function

Connection:
- Immune cells have cannabinoid receptors
- ECS modulates immune responses
- Involved in inflammation regulation
- Complex interactions

### Immunomodulation

What this means:
- Not simply "boosting" immunity
- Not simply suppressing it
- Balancing and regulating
- Context-dependent effects

### Anti-Inflammatory Effects

Important distinction:

---

## My Take

The "immune booster" marketing around CBD is oversimplified and often misleading. CBD doesn't boost immunity the way that phrase implies—it modulates immune function, which is more nuanced and context-dependent.

Where CBD may genuinely help is in managing overactive immune responses and chronic inflammation. For general wellness and illness prevention, the evidence is much less clear. Focus on proven immune support—good sleep, nutrition, exercise, stress management—and view CBD as potentially supportive, not as an immune supplement.
