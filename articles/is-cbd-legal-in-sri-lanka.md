# Is CBD Legal in Sri Lanka? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Sri Lanka**. Cannabis and all derivatives are prohibited under Sri Lankan law. The country maintains strict prohibition with no exemptions for CBD or medical cannabis.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Sri Lanka's stance:
- All cannabis prohibited
- Poisons, Opium and Dangerous Drugs Ordinance
- No CBD exemption
- No medical cannabis

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Imprisonment
- Fines
- Criminal record
- Deportation possible

## For Visitors

Critical:

- Enforcement applies to tourists
