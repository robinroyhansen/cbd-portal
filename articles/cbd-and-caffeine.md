# CBD and Caffeine: Can You Combine Them?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD and caffeine—one relaxing, one stimulating—might seem like opposites. Yet many people combine them, and CBD-infused coffee has become popular. Here's what happens when you mix these two compounds.

---

## The Logic of Combining

### Opposite Effects?

The theory:
- Caffeine stimulates and energizes
- CBD calms and relaxes
- Combined: "calm alertness"?
- Many report balanced effect

### Why People Combine

Common reasons:
- Reduce caffeine jitters
- Maintain focus without anxiety
- Balanced energy
- Enjoy coffee with CBD benefits

---

## What Happens When Combined

### Reported Effects

Users commonly report:
- Alert but not anxious
- Focused without jitters
- Smooth energy
- Less caffeine crash

### The Science

Limited research:
- No major studies on combination
- Both affect adenosine receptors
- Theoretical interactions exist
- Mostly anecdotal evidence

---

## How to Combine Them

### Adding CBD to Coffee

Methods:
- CBD oil drops in coffee
- CBD-infused coffee products
- Capsule taken with coffee
- Water-soluble CBD mixes better

### Timing

Approaches:
- Together (CBD in coffee)
- CBD first, coffee after
- Coffee first, CBD after
- Find what works for you

### Oil in Coffee Note

Practical issue:
- CBD oil is fat-based
- Floats on water/coffee
- May not mix perfectly
- Water-soluble CBD mixes better

---

## Dosing Considerations

### Balance

Finding sweet spot:
- Too much CBD may reduce alertness
- Too little may not counter jitters
- Experiment with ratios
- Start with lower CBD dose

### Typical Approach

Common doses:
- Regular coffee amount
- 10-20mg CBD
- Adjust based on response
- Lower CBD than typical standalone dose

---

## CBD Coffee Products

### What's Available

Products include:
- CBD-infused coffee grounds
- CBD coffee pods
- Pre-made CBD cold brew
- Add-in CBD coffee enhancers

### Quality Varies

Considerations:
- Check CBD concentration
- Third-party testing matters
- May be expensive per mg
- Convenience vs. DIY

---

## Potential Concerns

### Interactions

Consider:
- Both processed by liver
- Theoretically could affect each other's metabolism
- Unlikely to be significant at normal doses
- Monitor your response

### Not For Everyone

Skip if:
- Sensitive to either compound
- Taking medications that interact with either
- Experience adverse effects
- Advised against by doctor

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Combining is common** and generally considered safe
2. **May reduce jitters** while maintaining alertness
3. **Start with lower doses** of each
4. **Limited research** but lots of anecdotal support
5. **Water-soluble CBD** mixes better in coffee

---

## My Take

CBD coffee makes intuitive sense—the CBD takes the edge off caffeine's jittery effects while you stay alert and focused. Many people swear by this combination for productive mornings.

The research is lacking, but the combination appears safe for most people. If you're going to try it, start with less CBD than you'd normally take alone (maybe 10-15mg) and see how the balanced effect feels.
