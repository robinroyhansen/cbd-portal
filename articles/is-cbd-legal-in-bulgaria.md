# Is CBD Legal in Bulgaria? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Bulgaria** when derived from EU-approved industrial hemp with less than 0.2% THC. Bulgaria has a growing hemp industry and an emerging CBD market. Products are available in Sofia and other cities, with the domestic industry developing.

---

## Legal Framework

### Bulgarian CBD Regulations

Bulgaria's approach:
- EU member since 2007
- Industrial hemp cultivation legal
- THC limit: 0.2% (EU standard)
- CBD products permitted commercially
- Growing hemp production
- Novel Food regulations apply

### Bulgaria as Hemp Producer

Bulgaria has become a significant hemp country:
- Favorable climate
- Lower production costs
- Export-focused industry
- Growing processing capacity

## What's Legal

### Clearly Legal

- CBD oils with <0.2% THC
- CBD cosmetics and topicals
- Hemp food products
- CBD capsules and supplements

### Complex/Restricted

- CBD flowers (grey area)
- Products with health claims
- High-concentration products

### Prohibited

- THC above 0.2%
- Medical claims without authorization
- Sale to minors
