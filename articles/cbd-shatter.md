## Quick Summary

CBD shatter is a highly concentrated CBD extract with a glass-like, brittle texture that "shatters" when broken. It's typically 80-90%+ pure CBD, created through an extraction process that removes plant material, waxes, and fats. CBD shatter is primarily used through dabbing or vaporisation and offers a potent, fast-acting CBD experience.

## Key Takeaways

- Highly concentrated (80-90%+ CBD)
- Glass-like, brittle texture
- Consumed via dabbing/vaporising
- Very pure form of CBD
- For experienced users

## What Is CBD Shatter?

### Definition

CBD shatter is a type of CBD concentrate known for its hard, translucent, glass-like appearance. When handled, it breaks or "shatters" into pieces, hence the name.

### Characteristics

**Appearance:**
- Clear to amber colour
- Translucent
- Glass-like texture
- Breaks into shards

**Potency:**
- 80-99% cannabinoids typical
- Primarily CBD
- Very concentrated
- Small amounts needed

---

## How CBD Shatter Is Made

### Extraction Process

**1. Initial Extraction:**
- BHO (Butane Hash Oil) method common
- CO2 extraction also used
- CBD extracted from hemp

**2. Purging:**
- Solvents removed
- Vacuum purging
- Heat applied carefully

**3. Cooling:**
- Controlled cooling process
- Creates glass-like structure
- No agitation (creates shatter vs wax)

### Why It's Different from Other Concentrates

**Key Difference:**
The cooling process without agitation creates the glass-like structure. If agitated during cooling, it would become wax or crumble instead.

## CBD Shatter vs. Other Concentrates

### Comparison

| Concentrate | Texture | CBD % | Process Difference |
|-------------|---------|-------|-------------------|
| Shatter | Glass-like | 80-99% | No agitation cooling |
| Wax | Soft, waxy | 80-90% | Agitated during cooling |
| Crumble | Dry, crumbly | 80-90% | More agitation |
| Distillate | Thick liquid | 90-99% | Further distillation |

### Why Choose Shatter?

- Very pure
- Easy to handle
- Stable at room temperature
- Long shelf life
- Precise portioning

## How to Use CBD Shatter

### Dabbing

**Most Common Method:**
1. Heat dab nail/banger
2. Let cool slightly
3. Apply small amount of shatter
4. Inhale vapour
5. Use carb cap if desired

**Equipment Needed:**
- Dab rig
- Dab nail/banger
- Torch (or e-nail)
- Dab tool

### Vaporising

**Alternative Method:**
- Use concentrate-compatible vaporiser
- Set appropriate temperature
- Load small amount
- Inhale vapour

### Adding to Flower

**Combination Use:**
- Crumble shatter pieces
- Mix with CBD flower
- Smoke or vaporise
- Increases potency

## Dosing CBD Shatter

### Start Very Small

**Because It's Concentrated:**
- Tiny amounts needed
- Rice grain size or less
- 10-25mg CBD in small piece
- Observe before re-dosing

### Calculating

**Example:**
- Shatter is 85% CBD
- 50mg piece contains ~42.5mg CBD
- Much stronger than oil
- Adjust accordingly

### Potency Warning

CBD shatter is extremely concentrated. New users should start with minimal amounts and increase gradually.

## Proper Storage

### How to Store

**Best Practices:**
- Parchment paper or silicone container
- Cool, dark location
- Away from heat and light
- Airtight container

**What to Avoid:**
- Direct sunlight
- Heat sources
- Humidity
- Touching with fingers (oils)

### Shelf Life

- Months to years if stored well
- Quality degrades with heat/light
- Stable at room temperature
- Watch for colour changes
