# Is CBD Legal in Oman? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Oman**. The Sultanate prohibits all cannabis-derived products with strict penalties. No distinction is made between CBD and THC under Omani law. Do not bring CBD to Oman.

### Prohibition

Oman's position:
- All cannabis products banned
- CBD treated as controlled substance
- No medical cannabis program
- Strong anti-drug stance

### Penalties

Potential consequences:
- Imprisonment
- Heavy fines
- Deportation
- Travel restrictions
