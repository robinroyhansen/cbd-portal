# CBD for Glaucoma: What You Need to Know

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Glaucoma is a leading cause of blindness involving elevated eye pressure. Importantly, **CBD may NOT be helpful for glaucoma and could potentially be counterproductive**. Here's what the research shows.

---

## Understanding Glaucoma

### What It Is

Condition:
- Group of eye diseases
- Damage to optic nerve
- Often from elevated intraocular pressure (IOP)
- Can lead to blindness

### Treatment Goal

Essential:
- Lowering intraocular pressure
- Preventing further damage
- Preserving vision
- Regular monitoring

---

## CBD and Glaucoma: Concerning Research

### What Studies Show

Critical finding:
- CBD may INCREASE eye pressure
- One study showed CBD raised IOP
- Opposite of treatment goal
- Potentially harmful

### THC Is Different

Important distinction:
- THC may temporarily lower IOP
- But effect is short-lived
- Requires constant dosing
- Not practical for treatment

### CBD Specifically

Evidence:
- CBD alone may worsen glaucoma
- Can counteract THC's IOP-lowering effect
- Not recommended for glaucoma
- Standard treatments are better

---

## Medical Guidance

### What Ophthalmologists Say

Consensus:
- CBD not recommended for glaucoma
- Standard treatments effective
- Eye drops, medications, procedures available
- Don't substitute CBD

### If You Have Glaucoma

Important:
- Inform eye doctor if using CBD
- May affect your condition
- Continue prescribed treatments
- Regular monitoring essential

---

## Standard Glaucoma Treatments

### What Works

Options:
- Prescription eye drops
- Oral medications
- Laser treatments
- Surgery when needed

### Why They're Better

Compared to CBD:
- Proven IOP reduction
- Consistent effects
- Long-lasting
- Evidence-based

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD may increase** eye pressure
2. **Not recommended** for glaucoma
3. **Could be counterproductive**
4. **Standard treatments** are effective
5. **Tell your eye doctor** if using CBD

---

## My Take

This is one case where I have to clearly advise against CBD for the specific condition. Research suggests CBD may actually increase eye pressure—the opposite of what glaucoma treatment requires.

If you have glaucoma and are using CBD for other reasons, inform your ophthalmologist. They may need to monitor your eye pressure more closely. Don't use CBD expecting it to help glaucoma—it likely won't and may harm.
