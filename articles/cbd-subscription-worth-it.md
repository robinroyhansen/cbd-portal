# Are CBD Subscriptions Worth It? Pros, Cons, and Considerations

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Many CBD brands offer subscription services with regular deliveries and discounts. Whether a subscription makes sense depends on your usage patterns, the discount offered, and your commitment to a particular product. This guide helps you decide.

---

## How CBD Subscriptions Work

### Typical Features

Most subscriptions offer:
- Recurring delivery (monthly)
- Discount (10-30% typically)
- Auto-ship convenience
- Easy cancellation (usually)

### Common Models

Types:
- Single product subscription
- Build-your-own bundle
- Curated boxes
- Flexible frequency

---

## Pros of CBD Subscriptions

### 1. Cost Savings

Benefits:
- 10-30% discounts typical
- Savings compound over time
- Often free shipping
- Better than one-time purchases

### 2. Convenience

Advantages:
- Never run out
- No reordering hassle
- Arrives automatically
- Set and forget

### 3. Consistency

For regular users:
- Same product each time
- No variation in quality
- Maintains routine
- Reliable supply

---

## Cons of CBD Subscriptions

### 1. Commitment

Downsides:
- Locked into one brand
- May accumulate excess
- Need to remember to cancel
- Less flexibility

### 2. Product Fatigue

Considerations:
- May want to try other brands
- Stuck if needs change
- Limited experimentation
- Same thing every month

### 3. Potential Issues

Watch for:
- Difficult cancellation
- Unexpected charges
- Product changes without notice
- Poor customer service

---

## When Subscriptions Make Sense

### Good Candidates

Subscribe if you:
- Use CBD daily consistently
- Know which product works for you
- Value convenience
- Want to save money
- Found a brand you trust

### Bad Candidates

Skip subscriptions if you:
- Still experimenting
- Use CBD occasionally
- Like trying different products
- Have variable usage
- Uncertain about continued use

---

## Before Subscribing

### Questions to Ask

1. What's the exact discount?
2. How easy is cancellation?
3. Can I skip shipments?
4. What's the return policy?
5. Are there minimum commitments?

### Calculate Your Savings

Example:
- Monthly use: 1 bottle ($60)
- Subscription: $48 (20% off)
- Annual savings: $144
- Worth it? Consider factors

---

## Red Flags

### Avoid Subscriptions That

- Make cancellation difficult
- Have minimum terms
- Don't clearly show pricing
- Auto-charge without notice
- Have poor reviews about cancellation

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Subscriptions save 10-30%** typically
2. **Best for consistent users** who know what works
3. **Verify easy cancellation** before signing up
4. **Calculate actual savings** for your usage
5. **Skip if still experimenting**

---
