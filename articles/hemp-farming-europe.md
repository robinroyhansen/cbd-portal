# Hemp Farming in Europe: Industry Overview

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Europe has a growing hemp farming industry, with France, Italy, and Germany among the leading producers. EU regulations govern cultivation, requiring certified seed varieties and THC compliance. European hemp is often considered high-quality due to strict standards.

---

## Key Takeaways

- EU hemp cultivation regulated under CAP
- Certified seed varieties required
- THC limit 0.2% (moving to 0.3%)
- France leads in production area
- Growing interest in CBD cultivation

---

## EU Regulatory Framework

### Common Agricultural Policy (CAP)

**Key Requirements:**
- Cultivation permits/notifications
- Certified seed varieties only
- THC testing compliance
- Subsidy eligibility criteria
- Member state oversight

### THC Limits

**Current Standards:**
- EU limit: 0.2%
- Proposed increase to 0.3%
- Testing during flowering
- Consequences for non-compliance

### Certified Varieties

**EU Common Catalogue:**
- 75+ approved varieties
- Low THC guaranteed
- Traceable seeds
- Updated regularly

## Cultivation Methods

### For CBD Production

**Best Practices:**
