By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Summary

Para-cymene (p-cymene) is a monoterpene with a mild, pleasant, citrus-like aroma. Found in thyme, cumin, and cannabis, cymene shows potential anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. It's a precursor to other beneficial compounds like thymol.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Mild, pleasant citrus-like aroma
- Potential anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties
- Precursor to thymol (major thyme compound)
- Antioxidant activity
- Found in thyme, cumin, oregano, and cannabis

---

## What Is Cymene?

Para-cymene is a naturally occurring aromatic compound found in many essential oils. It's a precursor to other valuable terpenes and phenols, including thymol and carvacrol.

Natural sources:
- **Thyme** — Significant content
- **Cumin** — Spice contribution
- **Oregano** — Part of herb profile
- **Coriander** — Found in seeds
- **Cannabis** — Various strains
