# CBD for Phone Anxiety: Managing Call Reluctance

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Phone anxiety—the dread of making or receiving calls—has become increasingly common in the texting era. While it may seem minor, phone anxiety can significantly impact work and relationships. CBD may help reduce the anxiety that makes phone calls feel so difficult.

---

## Understanding Phone Anxiety

### What It Is

Characteristics:
- Dread before making calls
- Letting calls go to voicemail
- Extensive preparation for simple calls
- Physical anxiety symptoms
- Preferring any other communication

### Why It Happens

Causes:
- Real-time pressure to respond
- Can't edit your words
- Interpreting tone is harder
- Fear of awkward silences
- Less practice as texting increased

### Impact

Effects:
- Missed opportunities
- Work complications
- Relationship strain
- Avoiding important calls
- Increased overall anxiety

---

## How CBD Might Help

### Anxiety Reduction

Potential effects:
- Calms anticipatory dread
- Reduces physical symptoms
- Makes picking up phone easier
- Lowers barrier to calling

### What It Enables

With less anxiety:
- Quicker to return calls
- Less preparation needed
- More natural conversation
- Actually make that call

---

## Using CBD for Phone Anxiety

### For Important Calls

Approach:
- Take 30-45 minutes before planned call
- Allows time for effect
- Call when ready
- Reduced anxiety during conversation

### For General Anxiety

Daily approach:
- Consistent low-dose use
- Reduces overall anxiety
- Phone calls become less daunting
- Part of broader anxiety management

### Dosing

For phone anxiety:
- Low dose (10-20mg) often sufficient
- Stay clear-headed for conversation
- Test during low-stakes calls
- Find what works

---

## Building Phone Confidence

### With CBD Support

Practice:
- Make easier calls first
- Build up to harder ones
- Gradual exposure
- CBD takes edge off

### Techniques

Also helpful:
- Prepare talking points
- Stand while talking (more confident)
- Smile (affects voice)
- Remember they can't see you

### Long-term Goal

Working toward:
- Less anxiety over time
- CBD as support during building phase
- Eventually may need less
- Phone calls become normal

## Work-Related Calls

### Professional Context

For work:
- CBD before important calls
- Client calls, presentations
- Conference calls
- Job-related discussions

### Maintaining Professionalism

Balance:
- Stay sharp and focused
- Low dose only
- Test before important calls

## Key Takeaways

1. **Phone anxiety is real** and increasingly common
2. **CBD may reduce** the dread and physical symptoms

## My Take

Phone anxiety might seem trivial, but avoiding calls can genuinely hold you back. CBD can help lower the barrier—making it easier to just pick up the phone or make that call you've been putting off.

Use CBD to support gradual exposure to phone calls. Over time, you may find phone calls become less anxiety-provoking naturally.
