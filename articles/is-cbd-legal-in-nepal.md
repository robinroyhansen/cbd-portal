# Is CBD Legal in Nepal? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Nepal**. Despite the historical cannabis culture and past legal cultivation, all cannabis products including CBD are now prohibited. Do not bring CBD to Nepal.

---

## Legal Framework

### Current Status

Nepal's position:
- Cannabis prohibited since 1973
- CBD not distinguished from cannabis
- No medical cannabis program
- Prohibition enforced

### Historical Context

Background:
- Cannabis was legal until 1973
- Changed due to international pressure
- Historic hashish culture
- Now strictly prohibited
