# Terpineol: The Calming Lilac-Scented Terpene

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Alpha-terpineol is a monoterpene alcohol with a pleasant floral, lilac-like aroma. Known for sedative and relaxing properties, terpineol is commonly found in cannabis strains known for calming effects and is frequently used in aromatherapy and cosmetics.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Floral, lilac-like aroma with hints of pine
- Notable sedative and relaxing effects
- Strong antioxidant properties
- Antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory potential
- Found in lilacs, pine, eucalyptus, and cannabis

---

## What Is Terpineol?

Terpineol exists in four isomeric forms, with alpha-terpineol being most common and studied. It has a pleasant floral scent reminiscent of lilacs and lime blossoms.

Natural sources:
- **Lilacs** — Contributes to distinctive scent
- **Pine oil** — Industrial source
- **Eucalyptus** — Part of complex profile
- **Petitgrain** — Citrus tree derivation
- **Cannabis** — Various strains

## Terpineol's Effects and Benefits

### Sedative Properties

Research demonstrates:
- Significant sedative effects in animal studies
- May enhance sleep quality
- Often found in relaxing cannabis strains
- Complements myrcene and linalool

### Antioxidant Effects

Studies show:
- Strong antioxidant activity
- Cell protection from oxidative damage
- Potential anti-aging benefits

### Antimicrobial Activity

Research indicates:
- Antibacterial effects against various pathogens
- Antifungal properties
- Used in cleaning and cosmetic products

### Potential Anti-Inflammatory

Early research suggests:
- May reduce inflammatory markers
- Possible pain-relieving properties

## Terpineol in CBD Products

Found in:

## My Take

Terpineol is a lovely terpene for evening use. Its sedative effects are well-documented, and its pleasant floral aroma makes products enjoyable to use. Often overshadowed by linalool for calming effects, terpineol works alongside it to create genuinely relaxing experiences. If you find certain CBD products particularly calming, terpineol is likely contributing.
