By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Egypt**. Egypt has strict anti-drug laws, and all cannabis-derived products including CBD are prohibited. Penalties are severe. **Do not bring CBD to Egypt under any circumstances.**

---

## Egypt's Drug Laws

### Strict Prohibition

Egypt's position:
- All cannabis products illegal
- CBD not distinguished
- Zero tolerance policy
- Severe enforcement

---

## Penalties

Consequences:

- Trafficking: Life imprisonment or death
- Foreigners prosecuted
- No leniency
