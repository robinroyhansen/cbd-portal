# CBD and the Placebo Effect: Understanding the Relationship

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

The placebo effect—improvement from expecting a treatment to work—plays a role in CBD's perceived effects, as it does with all treatments. However, this doesn't mean CBD effects are imaginary. Placebo-controlled research shows CBD has effects beyond placebo for some conditions, while the placebo effect itself involves real neurobiological changes.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Placebo effects are real neurobiological phenomena
- CBD research uses placebo controls for this reason
- Some CBD effects exceed placebo in trials
- Expectations influence all treatment outcomes
- Placebo response doesn't mean "not working"

## CBD Research and Placebo Controls

### Why Controls Matter

**Placebo-Controlled Trials:**
- Compare CBD to inactive substance
- Account for placebo effect
- Isolate true drug effect
- Gold standard methodology

### What Research Shows

**Epidiolex (Epilepsy):**
- Significantly better than placebo
- 40%+ seizure reduction vs ~15% placebo
- Clear drug effect demonstrated
- FDA approval based on this

**Anxiety:**
- Some trials show CBD > placebo
- Effect sizes vary
- Promising but limited trials
- More research needed

**Pain:**
- Mixed results vs placebo
- Some positive trials
- Others no difference
- Unclear picture

---

## The Challenge of Blinding

### CBD Is Hard to Blind

**Issues:**
- Distinctive taste
- Side effects (fatigue, etc.)
- May tip off participants
- Affects validity

### Implications

- Some "CBD effects" may be expectation
- True effect harder to isolate
- Better blinding needed
- Interpretation challenges

## The Nocebo Effect

### Opposite of Placebo

**Definition:**
- Negative expectations causing harm
- Expecting side effects → experiencing them
- Real phenomenon
- Affects CBD users too

### Relevance

- Reading side effect lists may increase occurrence
- Negative attitudes may reduce effectiveness
- Expectations work both ways
- Context matters

## Research Perspective

### What Good Research Does

## My Take

The placebo effect is part of CBD's story, as it is for all treatments including pharmaceuticals. This doesn't diminish CBD's value—placebo effects are real benefits. The key is honest assessment: CBD clearly works beyond placebo for epilepsy, shows promising signals for anxiety, and has less clear advantages for other conditions. Understanding this helps set realistic expectations while still allowing for genuine benefits.
