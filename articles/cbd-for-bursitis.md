## Understanding Bursitis

### What It Is

Condition:
- Inflammation of bursae (joint cushions)
- Causes pain and swelling
- Often from overuse or pressure
- Common in middle age and older

### Common Locations

Affected areas:
- Shoulder (most common)
- Hip
- Elbow
- Knee
- Heel

### Symptoms

Experience:
- Joint area pain
- Swelling
- Stiffness
- Pain with movement
- Tenderness

## Using CBD for Bursitis

### Topical Application

Recommended:
- Apply to affected joint area
- Massage around (not directly on) if very tender
- Multiple times daily
- Generous application

### Oral CBD

Systemic support:
- Overall inflammation reduction
- 25-40mg typical
- Consistent daily use
- Supports topical application

### Combined Approach

Most effective:
- Topical for targeted relief
- Oral for systemic benefit
- Along with rest and other treatments
- Comprehensive strategy

---

## By Location

### Shoulder Bursitis

Approach:
- Topical around shoulder
- Avoid activities reaching overhead
- Ice therapy helpful
- Gradual movement restoration

### Hip Bursitis

Application:
- Topical to hip area
- Avoid lying on affected side
- Stretching when tolerable
- May need longer recovery

### Elbow Bursitis

Strategy:
- Topical to elbow
- Avoid leaning on elbow
- Padding if needed
- Often responds well to rest

---

## Complementary Treatments

### Standard Care

Essential:
- Rest from aggravating activities
- Ice (especially early)
- Anti-inflammatory approach
- Gentle movement when able

### Physical Therapy

Helpful for:
- Range of motion exercises
- Strengthening surrounding muscles
- Posture correction
- Activity modification

### Medical Treatment

When needed:
- Corticosteroid injections
- Aspiration (draining fluid)
- Antibiotics if infected
- Surgery (rarely)

## My Take

Bursitis is fundamentally an inflammatory condition, making CBD a logical complementary approach. Topical application to the affected joint area allows targeted delivery of anti-inflammatory compounds.

But bursitis also requires identifying and avoiding what caused it. If you keep doing the aggravating activity, CBD won't be enough. Rest, ice, and addressing the root cause are essential alongside CBD use.
