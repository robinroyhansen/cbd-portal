# High-CBD Hemp Strains: Popular Varieties

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

High-CBD hemp strains have been selectively bred to produce elevated cannabidiol levels while maintaining THC below legal limits. Popular strains like Cherry Wine, Elektra, and Lifter offer different terpene profiles and characteristics, influencing the effects and quality of CBD products.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Genetics determine CBD potential
- Terpene profiles vary by strain
- EU certified varieties required in Europe
- US market has more strain diversity
- Strain affects both potency and character

---

## Understanding Hemp Genetics

### Why Strains Matter

Different strains offer:
- Varying CBD concentrations
- Unique terpene profiles
- Different growing characteristics
- Distinct flavour profiles
- Various effect tendencies

### Breeding Goals

**Modern CBD Hemp:**
- High CBD content (15-25%+)
- Legal THC levels (<0.2-0.3%)
- Good yields
- Disease resistance
- Consistent results

## Popular High-CBD Strains

### Cherry Wine

**Profile:**
- CBD: 15-20%
- Terpenes: Myrcene, caryophyllene
- Flavour: Sweet, cherry notes
- Origin: US breeding

**Characteristics:**
- Balanced effects
- Pleasant aroma
- Good yields
- Popular choice

### Elektra

**Profile:**
- CBD: 15-20%
- Terpenes: Terpinolene, caryophyllene
- Flavour: Citrus, chocolate
- Origin: Oregon CBD

**Characteristics:**
- Uplifting terpene profile
- Complex flavour
- Good structure
- Farmer favourite

### Lifter

**Profile:**
- CBD: 16-20%
- Terpenes: Myrcene, limonene
- Flavour: Funky, tropical
- Origin: Oregon CBD

**Characteristics:**
- Energising profile
- Distinct aroma
- Strong yields
- Popular for extraction

### Suver Haze

**Profile:**
- CBD: 18-22%
- Terpenes: Myrcene, pinene
- Flavour: Pepper, fruit
- Origin: Oregon CBD

**Characteristics:**
- Relaxing tendency
- High potency
- Good resin production
- Quality extract source

### Special Sauce

**Profile:**
- CBD: 16-20%
- Terpenes: Myrcene, caryophyllene
- Flavour: Berry, earthy
- Origin: Oregon CBD

**Characteristics:**
- Calming effects
- Distinctive taste
- Good yields
- Versatile use

### Hawaiian Haze

**Profile:**
- CBD: 15-18%
- Terpenes: Limonene, pinene
- Flavour: Tropical, citrus
- Origin: Oregon CBD

**Characteristics:**
- Uplifting profile
- Bright flavour
- Sativa-like growth
- Popular smokable
