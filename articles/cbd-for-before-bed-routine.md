# CBD in Your Before-Bed Routine: Optimizing Sleep Preparation

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

A consistent bedtime routine signals your body to prepare for sleep. Adding CBD can enhance this routine, supporting relaxation and sleep onset. This guide shows how to integrate CBD effectively into your pre-sleep ritual.

---

## Why Bedtime Routines Matter

### Sleep Preparation

Benefits:
- Signals body sleep is coming
- Reduces time to fall asleep
- Improves sleep quality
- Creates predictable transition

### CBD's Role

How it helps:
- Supports relaxation process
- Reduces anxious thoughts
- Prepares nervous system
- Becomes part of the signal

---

## When to Take CBD

### Timing

Optimal window:
- 30-60 minutes before bed
- Allows time for onset
- Effects active when ready to sleep
- Consistent timing helps

### Why Timing Matters

Sleep preparation:
- CBD takes time to work
- Sublingual: 15-45 minutes
- Capsules/edibles: 45-90 minutes
- Plan accordingly

---

## Building the Routine

### Sample Evening Routine

Example schedule (60 min before bed):
1. Take CBD (set timer)
2. Dim lights
3. Personal hygiene
4. Light stretching or reading
5. Into bed

### Consistency Is Key

Important:
- Same time each night
- Same sequence of activities
- Body learns the pattern
- Sleep becomes easier

---

## Complementary Practices

### With CBD

Enhance effects:
- Dim or warm lighting
- No screens (or night mode)
- Calm activities
- Comfortable environment

### Good Pairings

Activities:
- Light stretching
- Reading (physical book)
- Meditation or breathing
- Journaling
- Warm bath/shower

### What to Avoid

Before bed:
- Bright screens
- Intense exercise
- Work or stressful tasks
- Heavy meals
- Caffeine

## Common Mistakes

### What Not to Do

Avoid:
- Taking CBD and expecting instant sleep
- Irregular timing
- Continuing stimulating activities
- Screens after CBD
- Inconsistent use

### Better Approaches

Instead:
- Give CBD time to work
- Consistent timing
- Transition activities
- Screen cutoff before CBD
- Daily use

---

## Troubleshooting

### If Not Working

Consider:
- Is timing right?
- Dose sufficient?
- Other factors interfering?
- Product quality okay?
- Give it 1-2 weeks of consistency

### Adjustments

Try:
- Earlier timing
- Higher dose
- Different product
- Better sleep hygiene
- Address underlying issues
