# CBD Subscription Services: Are They Worth It?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Many CBD brands offer subscription services with automatic delivery and discounts. This guide helps you evaluate whether subscribing makes sense for your situation and what to look for in a CBD subscription.

---

## How CBD Subscriptions Work

### Basic Model

Typical structure:
- Choose product and frequency
- Automatic billing and shipping
- Usually discounted (10-30% off)
- Cancel or modify anytime (usually)

### Common Options

What brands offer:
- Monthly delivery most common
- Every 2-3 months also available
- Quantity adjustments
- Product swapping options

---

## Potential Benefits

### Cost Savings

Financial advantage:
- 10-30% discount typical
- Adds up over time
- Bulk savings without bulk buying
- Consistent pricing

### Convenience

Practical benefits:
- Never run out
- No reordering hassle
- Automatic delivery
- One less thing to think about

### Consistency

Usage benefit:
- Encourages regular use
- Consistent supply
- Better results from consistent use
- No gaps in routine

---

## Potential Drawbacks

### Commitment Pressure

Concerns:
- May feel locked in
- Unused product accumulates
- Cancellation may be difficult
- Psychological pressure to continue

### Limited Flexibility

Restrictions:
- Stuck with one product
- Can't try competitors
- May miss better options
- Less experimentation

### Overstock Risk

Practical issue:
- Life changes affect usage
- Product can expire
- Accumulation if not adjusted
- Wasted money

---

## When Subscriptions Make Sense

### Good Candidates

Consider if:
- You use CBD daily consistently
- You've found your ideal product
- Discount is significant (15%+)
- Easy cancellation policy

### Not Ideal If

Skip if:
- Still experimenting with products
- Inconsistent usage
- Small discount (under 10%)
- Difficult cancellation terms

---

## Evaluating Subscription Offers

### Check the Discount

Compare:
- What's the actual savings?
- Compare to one-time purchase price
- Factor in shipping savings
- Calculate annual savings

### Cancellation Policy

Critical:
- Can you cancel anytime?
- Online or phone required?
- Any fees for canceling?
- Pause options available?

### Flexibility

Important features:
- Change products?
- Adjust frequency?
- Skip deliveries?
- Modify quantities?

### Quality Consistency

Consider:
- Is product quality consistent?
- Do they change formulations?
- Same third-party testing?
- Reliable supply?

---

## Managing a Subscription

### Track Usage

Monitor:
- Match delivery to actual use
- Adjust frequency as needed
- Don't let product accumulate
- Review regularly

### Regular Review

Best practice:
- Assess every few months
- Still the best product?
- Better options available?
- Worth the discount?

### Know When to Cancel

Signs:
- Product accumulating unused
- Found better alternative
- Needs changed
- Better deals elsewhere

---

## Alternatives to Subscriptions

### Bulk Buying

Option:
- Buy larger quantities at discount
- More control over timing
- Often similar savings
- No commitment

### Sales and Promotions

Strategy:
- Stock up during sales
- Follow brands for deals
- Holiday promotions
- Can exceed subscription savings

### Loyalty Programs

Some brands offer:
- Points for purchases
- Rewards without commitment
- Flexible redemption
- No auto-billing

---

## Red Flags

### Watch For

Warning signs:
- Discount too good (quality concern)
- Difficult cancellation
- Hidden fees
- Poor customer service

### Avoid If

Skip subscriptions with:
- Cancellation fees
- Phone-only cancellation
- Unclear terms
- Frequent product changes

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Subscriptions work** for consistent, daily users
2. **Discounts should be** significant (15%+)
3. **Cancellation should be** easy and free
4. **Review regularly** to ensure value
5. **Not ideal** for those still experimenting

---
