# CBD Before Bed: Creating an Effective Night Routine

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Using CBD before bed requires the right timing, dose, and routine. This guide covers how to effectively incorporate CBD into your nighttime ritual for better sleep, including optimal timing, product choices, and complementary practices.

---

## Why CBD Before Bed

### Sleep Benefits

CBD may help:
- Reduce racing thoughts
- Ease physical tension
- Support sleep quality
- Promote relaxation
- Reduce anxiety affecting sleep

### Not a Sedative

Important note:
- CBD doesn't "knock you out"
- Creates conditions for sleep
- Not like sleeping pills
- Works differently

---

## Optimal Timing

### When to Take

Best timing:
- 1-2 hours before bed
- After dinner (absorption)
- Same time consistently
- Not right at bedtime

### Why Not Right at Bedtime

Reasoning:
- Effects take time to onset
- Sublingual: 15-30 min
- Oral: 1-2 hours
- Plan accordingly

---

## Building the Routine

### Sample Night Routine

**8:00 PM:** Take CBD (1-2 hours before bed)
**8:30 PM:** Begin wind-down activities
**9:00 PM:** Reduce screen time
**9:30 PM:** Relaxing activity (reading, bath)
**10:00 PM:** Bed

### Consistency Matters

Why routine helps:
- Body learns the signals
- CBD effects build
- Sleep drive aligns
- Better results over time

---

## Product Choices

### For Sleep

Best options:
- CBD oil (sublingual)
- CBD capsules (convenient)
- CBD gummies (tasty, slower)
- Avoid vapes before bed

### Formulations

Enhanced options:
- CBD + CBN (sleep-focused)
- CBD + melatonin (combined)
- Plain CBD (purist approach)

### Dosing for Sleep

Suggestions:
- Start: 25-50mg
- Higher than daytime often
- Adjust based on results
- Find minimum effective

---

## Complementary Practices

### Pair CBD With

Good sleep hygiene:
- Consistent bedtime
- Dark, cool room
- Screen limits
- No caffeine late
- Relaxation activities

### What Enhances CBD

Activities:
- Gentle stretching
- Meditation/breathing
- Warm bath
- Reading (not screens)
- Calming tea

---

## Troubleshooting

### If It's Not Working

Check:
- Timing (too close to bed?)
- Dose (too low?)
- Product quality
- Sleep hygiene issues
- Expectations realistic?

### Adjustments

Try:
- Earlier timing
- Higher dose
- Different product
- Better sleep hygiene
- Patience (2+ weeks)

## My Take

CBD before bed works best as part of a routine, not a last-minute addition. Taking it 1-2 hours before bed gives time for effects to onset, and pairing it with wind-down activities signals your body that sleep is coming.

Don't expect CBD to force you to sleep like a sedative. It helps create the calm conditions that let natural sleep happen.
