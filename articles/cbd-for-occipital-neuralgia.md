## Quick Summary

Occipital neuralgia causes sharp, shooting pain from the base of the skull up through the scalp. CBD may help with this nerve pain condition through anti-inflammatory and neuropathic pain effects. Here's what we know.

---

## Understanding Occipital Neuralgia

### What It Is

Condition:
- Irritation or injury to occipital nerves
- Nerves at base of skull
- Causes distinctive pain pattern
- Often confused with other headaches

### Symptoms

Experience:
- Sharp, shooting pain from skull base
- Pain radiates up scalp
- One or both sides
- Tender scalp
- Pain behind eyes sometimes
- Light sensitivity possible

### Causes

Triggers:
- Muscle tension compressing nerves
- Trauma/injury
- Prolonged head-down position
- Arthritis
- Sometimes unknown

## Using CBD for Occipital Neuralgia

Approach:

- Multiple times daily

### Oral CBD

- Consistent daily use

## Complementary Treatments

### Self-Care

Helpful:
- Heat to neck/skull base
- Gentle stretching
- Posture improvement
- Stress reduction

### Medical Options

Available:
- Nerve blocks
- Physical therapy
- Medications for nerve pain
- Botox injections
- Surgery (rarely)

### When to Seek Care

See a doctor:
- For proper diagnosis
- Rule out other causes
- If pain is severe
- If symptoms worsen
