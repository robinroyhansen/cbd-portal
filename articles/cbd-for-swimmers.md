## Quick Summary

Swimming is demanding on shoulders, provides full-body workout, and often involves early morning or double sessions. CBD may help swimmers with recovery, shoulder health, and sleep management around challenging training schedules.

## Swimming-Specific Benefits

### Why Swimmers Use CBD

Common reasons:
- Shoulder recovery and inflammation
- Full-body muscle soreness
- Sleep management (early practices)
- Pre-competition anxiety

### Unique Swimming Demands

CBD addresses:
- High-volume shoulder use
- Repetitive motion strain
- Sleep disruption from schedules
- Chlorine-related skin issues

## Dosing for Swimmers

### Training Phases

Adjust by phase:
- Base training: 20-30mg
- Hard training blocks: 40-60mg
- Taper: maintain or reduce
- Competition: personal choice

### Timing

Optimal schedule:

- Evening for sleep support
- Topical as needed
- Before early practices (some find helpful)
