# CBD Softgels vs Capsules: What's the Difference?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD softgels and capsules are both oral CBD formats, but they differ in construction. Softgels have a liquid CBD fill inside a soft, one-piece gelatin shell, while capsules are hard two-piece shells containing CBD powder or oil. Softgels may offer slightly better absorption and are easier to swallow, but capsules are often more affordable and have more vegan options.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Softgels: liquid fill, one-piece shell
- Capsules: powder or oil, two-piece shell
- Softgels often easier to swallow
- Capsules often more affordable
- Absorption may favour softgels slightly

---

## Understanding the Difference

### CBD Softgels

**Construction:**
- One-piece sealed shell
- Soft, pliable exterior
- Liquid CBD oil inside
- Gelatin or vegetarian shell

**Appearance:**
- Oval or oblong shape
- Smooth, shiny surface
- Often transparent or semi-transparent
- Can see liquid inside

### CBD Capsules

**Construction:**
- Two-piece shell
- Hard exterior
- CBD powder or oil inside
- Often opaque

**Appearance:**
- Cylindrical shape
- Matte surface
- Usually opaque
- Standard supplement look

## How Each Works

### Softgel Absorption

**Process:**
1. Swallowed
2. Shell dissolves in stomach
3. Liquid CBD released
4. Already in oil form for absorption
5. Processed through digestive system

**Advantage:**
CBD already dissolved in oil may absorb more readily.

### Capsule Absorption

**Process:**
1. Swallowed
2. Shell opens in stomach/intestine
3. Contents released
4. If powder, needs to dissolve
5. Then processed through digestive system

**Consideration:**
Extra step if CBD needs to dissolve first.

## Absorption Differences

### Do Softgels Absorb Better?

**Potentially:**
- Liquid form is ready for absorption
- No dissolution step needed
- Oil format aids CBD absorption
- Some studies suggest advantage

**However:**
- Difference may be modest
- Both undergo first-pass metabolism
- Individual variation matters
- Quality of CBD matters more

## Swallowing Experience

### Softgels

**Easier to Swallow:**
- Smooth, soft exterior
- Slides down easily
- Pliable shell
- Often smaller for same dose

### Capsules

**May Be Harder:**
- Hard shell can feel bulky
- May stick in throat
- Less smooth surface
- Adequate water helps

### For Those Who Struggle with Pills

Softgels are generally the better choice.

## Formulation Options

### Softgels Can Contain

**Formulation Options:**
- CBD oil with carrier
- Added ingredients in liquid
- Flavoured oils (internally)
- Various concentrations

### Capsules Can Contain

**Formulation Options:**
- CBD oil
- CBD powder/isolate
- Multiple ingredients
- Various concentrations

### Combination Products

Both can include additional ingredients like melatonin, vitamins, or other supplements.

## Dietary Considerations

### Vegan/Vegetarian

**Softgels:**
- Traditional: gelatin (not vegan)
- Some vegetarian options (harder to find)
- Fewer vegan-certified options

**Capsules:**
- Vegetable cellulose common
- Many vegan options
- Easier to find plant-based

### For Vegans

Capsules are usually the better choice.

## Stability and Shelf Life

### Softgels

**Stability:**
- Sealed liquid inside
- Good protection from air
- Stable if stored properly
- Can be affected by heat

### Capsules

**Stability:**
- Less airtight (two pieces)
- Powder more stable than liquid
- Generally good shelf life
- Less heat sensitive

### Overall

Both have adequate shelf stability when stored properly.

## My Take

The difference between softgels and capsules is mostly about convenience and preference rather than effectiveness. Softgels are easier to swallow and may absorb slightly better due to the liquid formulation, but capsules work well and often offer better value and more vegan options. Unless you specifically struggle with swallowing pills, the format matters less than the CBD quality inside. Choose based on what fits your needs—both deliver CBD effectively.
