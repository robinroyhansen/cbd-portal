## Safety Considerations

### Hydration Critical

Important:
- Sauna causes significant sweating
- CBD may cause dry mouth
- Drink water before, during, after
- Electrolytes help

### Heat Tolerance

Awareness:
- CBD enhances relaxation
- May make heat feel more intense
- Monitor how you feel
- Exit if uncomfortable

### Blood Pressure

Considerations:
- Sauna affects blood pressure
- CBD may lower blood pressure
- Combined effect possible
- Those with BP issues should consult doctor

### Alcohol Warning

Avoid:
- Never combine alcohol with sauna
- CBD + sauna + alcohol risky
- Dehydration compound
- Heat + alcohol dangerous

## Sauna Types

### Traditional Sauna

High heat:
- 150-195°F (65-90°C)
- Shorter sessions
- More intense
- Traditional Finnish style

### Infrared Sauna

Lower heat:
- 120-140°F (49-60°C)
- Longer sessions possible
- Penetrating heat
- Popular modern option

### CBD Works With Both

Compatibility:
- Same benefits with either
- Adjust session length
- Same protocols apply
- Personal preference
