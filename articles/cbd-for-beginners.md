## CBD Basics

### What Is CBD?

CBD (cannabidiol) is a compound from the cannabis plant. Key points:

- **Non-intoxicating** — CBD doesn't get you high
- **From hemp** — Legal CBD comes from hemp plants
- **Interacts with your body** — Works with the endocannabinoid system
- **Various forms** — Oils, gummies, capsules, topicals

### What CBD Is NOT

- Not marijuana (though from same plant family)
- Not psychoactive in the "high" sense
- Not a cure for anything
- Not regulated like pharmaceuticals
- Not guaranteed to work for everyone

### Legal Status

In most places:
- Hemp-derived CBD with <0.3% THC is legal
- Check your specific location
- Regulations vary by country/state

## Choosing Your First Product

### Product Types

**CBD Oil (Tinctures)**
- Taken under tongue
- Flexible dosing
- Moderate onset (15-45 min)
- Good for beginners

**Gummies/Edibles**
- Easy to take
- Pre-measured doses
- Slower onset (30-90 min)
- Tasty but more expensive per mg

**Capsules**
- Familiar pill format
- Consistent dosing
- Slower onset
- Convenient

**Topicals (Creams/Balms)**
- Applied to skin
- For localized issues
- No systemic effects
- Good for muscle/joint concerns

### My Recommendation for Beginners

**Start with CBD oil.** Why?
- Adjustable dosing
- Moderately fast onset
- Can increase/decrease easily
- Cost-effective
- Works sublingually (better absorption)

### Spectrum Types

| Type | Contains | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Full-Spectrum** | All cannabinoids including trace THC | Maximum benefit (entourage effect) |
| **Broad-Spectrum** | Multiple cannabinoids, no THC | Those wanting no THC |
| **Isolate** | Pure CBD only | Drug testing concerns, CBD-only |

**For most beginners:** Broad-spectrum offers a good balance.

## How to Choose a Quality Product

### Must-Haves

1. **Third-Party Lab Testing** — Certificate of Analysis (COA) available
2. **Clear CBD Content** — Know exactly how much you're getting
3. **THC Level Verified** — Especially if drug testing is a concern
4. **Reputable Brand** — Established company with good reviews
5. **Ingredient Transparency** — Full ingredient list available

### Red Flags

- No lab reports available
- Extremely cheap prices
- Health claims ("cures X")
- Unclear CBD amounts
- No company information

### How to Read a Lab Report (COA)

Look for:
- **Cannabinoid content** — CBD amount matches label
- **THC content** — Below legal limit or non-detect
- **Contaminant testing** — Pesticides, heavy metals, solvents
- **Recent date** — Within the past year

## How Much to Take

### Starting Dose

**Begin low:** 10-15mg CBD

**Why start low?**
- See how you respond
- Minimize chance of side effects
- Can always increase
- Find your minimum effective dose

### How to Increase

1. Start at 10-15mg
2. Use this dose for 3-5 days
3. Note any effects
4. If needed, increase by 5-10mg
5. Repeat until you find your dose

### Typical Dose Ranges

| Purpose | Common Range |
|---------|--------------|
| Mild support | 10-25mg |
| Moderate support | 25-50mg |
| More significant support | 50-100mg |

Everyone is different—these are guidelines, not rules.

### Calculating Doses

**For CBD oil:**
- Check mg per ml on bottle
- Most droppers hold about 1ml
- Divide total CBD by ml to get mg per dropper

**Example:**
- 30ml bottle with 1000mg CBD
- 1000 ÷ 30 = ~33mg per dropper
- Half dropper = ~16mg

## How to Take CBD

### Sublingual (Under Tongue)

Best absorption method for oils:
1. Draw up your dose
2. Place drops under tongue
3. Hold for 60-90 seconds
4. Swallow remainder

This allows absorption through mouth tissues.

### With Food

- Take CBD with fatty food
- Increases absorption significantly
- Meals or fatty snacks work well

### Timing

- **Sleep:** 1-2 hours before bed
- **Anxiety:** Morning or before stressful events
- **Pain:** Morning and/or evening
- **General:** Whatever fits your routine

## What to Expect

### First Time Taking CBD

You might notice:
- Subtle calm or relaxation
- Slight dry mouth
- Nothing obvious (common)

You probably won't notice:
- Dramatic instant effects
- A "high" or intoxication
- Immediate symptom relief

### Over Time

With consistent use:
- Effects often become more noticeable
- Optimal dose becomes clearer
- Results improve for some
- May need dose adjustments

### How Long Until It Works?

| Method | Onset Time |
|--------|------------|
| Sublingual oil | 15-45 minutes |
| Gummies/Edibles | 30-90 minutes |
| Capsules | 30-90 minutes |
| Topicals | 15-30 minutes (local) |

Full benefits may take 2-4 weeks of regular use.

## Common Beginner Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Dose Too Low

Many beginners use 5mg and expect miracles. Most people need at least 20-30mg to notice anything.

**Fix:** Don't be afraid to gradually increase.

### Mistake 2: Giving Up Too Soon

Trying CBD once or twice isn't enough.

**Fix:** Commit to consistent use for 2-4 weeks.

### Mistake 3: Poor Quality Product

That cheap CBD might contain little actual CBD.

**Fix:** Buy from reputable brands with lab testing.

### Mistake 4: Wrong Expectations

CBD isn't a cure-all miracle supplement.

**Fix:** Expect subtle support, not transformation.

### Mistake 5: Inconsistent Use

Random dosing makes it hard to judge effectiveness.

**Fix:** Same dose, same time, daily for weeks.

## Side Effects

### Common (Usually Mild)

- Dry mouth
- Drowsiness
- Appetite changes
- Mild GI upset

### What to Do

- Side effects are usually dose-related
- Lower your dose if problematic
- Take with food if GI issues
- Adjust timing if drowsiness is an issue

### When to Stop

- Significant discomfort
- Allergic reaction (rare)
- Concerning symptoms
- Before discussing with doctor

## Drug Interactions

### Important to Know

CBD can interact with medications:
- Affects how liver processes drugs
- Can increase or decrease drug levels
- Similar to grapefruit warning

### If You Take Medications

- Consult your pharmacist or doctor
- Especially important for:
  - Blood thinners
  - Heart medications
  - Anti-seizure drugs
  - Antidepressants

## My Take

As someone who's worked in CBD for over a decade, here's my honest advice for beginners:

Start simple. Get a quality broad-spectrum oil from a reputable brand. Take 15-20mg under your tongue with breakfast, every day, for three weeks. Track how you feel.

Don't overthink it. Don't buy five different products to start. Don't obsess over the "perfect" dose. Just begin, be consistent, and pay attention.

You might love it. You might find it helpful for one thing but not another. You might decide it's not for you. All of those outcomes are fine—you just need to actually try it properly to find out.

The biggest mistake I see is people taking too little, for too short a time, with a poor-quality product, then concluding "CBD doesn't work." Give it a fair shot first.
