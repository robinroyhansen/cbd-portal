# Is CBD Legal in Malta? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Malta** when derived from EU-approved industrial hemp containing less than 0.2% THC. Malta has become notably progressive on cannabis policy, having legalized recreational cannabis in 2021—the first EU country to do so. CBD products are widely available.
