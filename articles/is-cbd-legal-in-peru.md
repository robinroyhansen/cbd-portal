# Is CBD Legal in Peru? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Peru** for medical use. Peru legalized medical cannabis in 2017 and has developed regulations for CBD products. Consumer access is possible through pharmacies and medical channels.
