# Does CBD Cause Headaches?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Headaches are not a common CBD side effect, but some people do report them. Possible causes include low-quality products containing contaminants, dehydration, individual sensitivity, or withdrawal from other substances. Quality products taken properly rarely cause headaches.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Headaches are uncommon side effect
- Product quality often the culprit
- Dehydration can contribute
- Usually not the CBD itself
- Investigate cause if it happens

---

## Is Headache a Common Side Effect?

### Clinical Trial Data

**In Major Studies:**
- Headache not among top side effects
- Drowsiness, appetite changes more common
- When reported, usually mild
- Less common than other effects

### Real-World Reports

- Some users do report headaches
- Often correlates with product quality
- Individual variation exists
- Worth investigating if occurs

---

## Possible Causes

### Product Quality Issues

**Low-Quality CBD May Contain:**
- Heavy metals
- Pesticide residues
- Solvents
- Additives
- These can cause headaches

### Dehydration

**CBD and Hydration:**
- May cause dry mouth
- Some forget to hydrate
- Dehydration causes headaches
- Simple fix: drink more water

### Dose Issues

**Too Much:**
- High doses more likely
- Individual sensitivity
- Try reducing dose
- See if headache resolves

### Carrier Oil Sensitivity

**Some React To:**
- MCT oil
- Hemp seed oil
- Additives/flavourings
- Try different carrier

## Troubleshooting CBD Headaches

### If You Get Headaches

1. **Check product quality** — Is it tested?
2. **Increase hydration** — Drink more water
3. **Lower dose** — Try half
4. **Try different brand** — Quality varies
5. **Different carrier oil** — May be sensitivity
6. **Rule out other causes** — Unrelated issue?

### Quality Verification

- Check COA for contaminants
- Verify third-party testing
- Research brand reputation
- Consider premium products

## When to Be Concerned

### Stop CBD If

- Severe headaches
- Headaches every time
- Other concerning symptoms
- Pattern clearly correlates

### See Healthcare Provider If

- Persistent headaches
- Severe or unusual
- Accompanied by other symptoms
- Doesn't resolve with adjustments

## Prevention Strategies

### Best Practices

- Start with quality products
- Stay well hydrated
- Begin with low doses
- Track your response
- Don't ignore patterns

## My Take

In my experience, CBD causing headaches usually points to product quality issues rather than CBD itself. Quality, tested products from reputable brands rarely cause headaches. If you're experiencing them, investigate the product, check hydration, and try a different brand before concluding CBD isn't for you.
