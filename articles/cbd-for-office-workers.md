## Using CBD at Work

### Timing Considerations

Options:
- Morning dose before work
- Evening dose for sleep
- Lunchtime for afternoon
- Know your response first

### Keeping It Professional

Guidelines:
- Keep doses low during work hours
- Avoid sedation
- Know how it affects you
- Be discreet if needed

### If Drug Tested

For office workers tested:
- THC-free products only
- Verify with lab reports
- Know company policy

## Specific Applications

### For Work Stress

Approach:
- Consistent daily dosing
- Lower doses (10-20mg)
- Morning routine
- Don't expect miracles

### For Desk-Related Discomfort

CBD use:
- Oral for systemic effect
- Topical for back/neck
- After-work routine
- Combined with movement

### For Better Sleep

To improve work performance:
- Evening dosing
- 1-2 hours before bed
- Better rest = better focus
- Addresses stress-sleep cycle

## Ergonomics Still Matter

### CBD + Good Setup

Foundation:
- Proper chair
- Monitor height
- Regular breaks
- Movement throughout day
- CBD complements, doesn't replace
