# CBD During Pregnancy: Safety Concerns

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

**CBD is not recommended during pregnancy.** Major health organizations advise against using CBD while pregnant due to insufficient safety data and potential risks. Here's what you need to know.

---

## Official Recommendations

### FDA Position

Clear guidance:
- FDA strongly advises against CBD during pregnancy
- Concerns about fetal development
- Insufficient safety data
- Applies to all CBD products

### Medical Organizations

Consensus:
- ACOG (American College of OB/GYN) advises against
- WHO expresses concern
- No medical body recommends CBD in pregnancy
- Precautionary principle applies

---

## Why the Concern?

### Lack of Safety Data

The problem:
- No clinical trials on pregnant women
- Can't ethically conduct such trials
- Limited understanding of effects
- Unknown risks to fetus

### Potential Concerns

Theoretical risks:
- CBD crosses placenta
- May affect fetal brain development
- Endocannabinoid system role in pregnancy
- Unknown long-term effects on child

### Product Quality Issues

Additional concern:
- Many products contain undisclosed THC
- Contaminants possible
- Quality control varies
- THC known to affect fetal development

---

## Common Pregnancy Symptoms

### What Women Want Help With

Understandable:
- Morning sickness/nausea
- Anxiety
- Sleep disruption
- Pain and discomfort

### Safer Alternatives

Discuss with doctor:
- Ginger for nausea
- Safe sleep hygiene practices
- Prenatal yoga/exercise
- Approved medications when needed

---

## What If You Used CBD Before Knowing You Were Pregnant?

### Realistic Perspective

Understanding:
- Don't panic
- Discuss with your OB/GYN
- Single exposure likely low risk
- Stop use once pregnancy confirmed
- Most important is ongoing avoidance

---

## The Bottom Line

### Clear Recommendation

Advice:
- Do not use CBD during pregnancy
- Applies to all CBD products
- Risk not worth potential benefit
- Safer alternatives exist

### After Pregnancy

Note:
- Breastfeeding concerns also exist
- CBD transfers to breast milk
- Same caution applies
- Discuss timeline with doctor

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD not recommended** during pregnancy
2. **Insufficient safety data** exists
3. **All major health organizations** advise against
4. **Product quality** adds additional risk
5. **Talk to your doctor** about safe alternatives
