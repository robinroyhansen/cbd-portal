## Quick Summary

Stage fright—the anxiety before performing—affects musicians, actors, speakers, and presenters. Research on CBD for performance anxiety shows promise. Understanding how to use CBD for stage fright can help manage those pre-performance nerves.

---

## Understanding Stage Fright

### What It Is

Characteristics:
- Anxiety before/during performance
- Fear of judgment or failure
- Physical symptoms
- Can affect anyone
- Even experienced performers

### Physical Symptoms

Common experiences:
- Racing heart
- Sweating, trembling hands
- Dry mouth
- Voice changes
- Nausea, butterflies
- Mind going blank

### Impact

Effects:
- Avoids performance opportunities
- Underperforms relative to ability
- Career limitations
- Significant distress
- Can worsen over time

## Research on CBD and Performance Anxiety

### Key Studies

Evidence includes:
- Public speaking anxiety research
- Simulated speaking task studies
- Brain imaging studies
- Comparison with placebo

### Findings

What research shows:
- Reduced subjective anxiety
- Lower physiological symptoms
- Maintained cognitive function
- Non-sedating at appropriate doses

### Relevance to Stage Fright

Application:
- Similar anxiety response
- Performance under observation
- Fear of judgment component
- Physical symptoms overlap

## Using CBD for Stage Fright

### Timing

Critical factor:
- Take 30-60 minutes before performance
- Allow effects to onset
- Don't wait until last minute
- Know your personal timing

### Dosing

Performance context:
- Low-moderate dose (15-30mg typical)
- Avoid sedation
- Alertness essential
- Less is often more

### Test First

Essential:
- Never first-time use before important performance
- Practice during rehearsals
- Know exactly how it affects you
- Dial in your dose beforehand

Stage fright can hold talented performers back. The research on CBD for public speaking anxiety translates well to performance situations—it takes the edge off without dulling your abilities.
