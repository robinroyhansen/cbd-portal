# CBD for Surgery Recovery: What to Know

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Surgery recovery involves pain management, healing, and returning to function. CBD may help with some recovery aspects, but timing around surgery is critical. Always discuss with your surgical team.

---

## Important: Before Surgery

### Stop CBD Before Surgery

Critical:
- CBD affects liver enzymes
- May interact with anesthesia
- Can affect bleeding (possibly)
- Follow surgeon's instructions

### Typical Guidance

Recommendations:
- Stop CBD at least 2 weeks before surgery
- Disclose all supplement use
- Follow specific instructions from surgeon
- Resume only when cleared

---

## After Surgery: Potential Benefits

### Pain Management

Post-surgical:
- CBD may complement pain management
- Anti-inflammatory properties
- Reduces opioid reliance potentially
- Discuss with surgical team

### Sleep Support

Recovery essential:
- Sleep is crucial for healing
- CBD supports sleep quality
- May help despite discomfort
- Rest aids recovery

### Anxiety

Post-surgical stress:
- Recovery can be anxious time
- CBD may help manage stress
- Supports overall wellbeing
- Part of recovery support

---

## When to Resume CBD

### After Surgery

Timing:
- Wait for surgical team clearance
- Usually several days minimum
- Depends on surgery type
- Follow specific guidance

### Factors

Consider:
- Anesthesia fully cleared
- Not interacting with post-op medications
- Pain medication interactions
- Healing progress

---

## Drug Interactions

### Post-Surgical Medications

Watch for:
- Opioid pain medications
- Blood thinners
- Anti-inflammatories
- Antibiotics
- Other post-op medications

### Consult Your Team

Essential:
- Ask about specific interactions
- Get clearance before resuming
- Adjust timing if needed
- Safety first

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Stop CBD** before surgery (typically 2 weeks)
2. **Disclose use** to surgical team
3. **May help** recovery (pain, sleep, anxiety)
4. **Resume only** with clearance
5. **Watch for** drug interactions
