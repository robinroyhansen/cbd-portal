# CBD for Hangover: Can It Help?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Hangovers are unpleasant but common after excessive alcohol consumption. Some people use CBD as part of hangover recovery, citing potential benefits for nausea, headache, and general discomfort. Here's an honest look at what CBD can and can't do for hangovers.

---

## What Causes Hangovers

### The Physiology

Hangover factors:
- Dehydration
- Inflammation
- Electrolyte imbalance
- Acetaldehyde toxicity
- Sleep disruption

### Symptoms

Common experiences:
- Headache
- Nausea
- Fatigue
- Sensitivity to light/sound
- Brain fog
- Muscle aches

---

## How CBD Might Help

### Potential Benefits

CBD may address:
- Nausea (anti-emetic research)
- Inflammation
- General discomfort
- Anxiety component

### What Research Suggests

Evidence:
- Anti-inflammatory properties
- Nausea research exists
- General wellness support
- No hangover-specific studies

---

## Using CBD for Hangovers

### Timing

Options:
- Morning after (reactive)
- Before bed after drinking (preventive)
- Both approaches used

### Delivery Methods

For hangovers:
- Sublingual oil (faster, doesn't need digestion)
- Capsules (if stomach can handle)
- Water-soluble (mixes with water)
- Avoid anything requiring active digestion if very nauseous

### Dosing

Approach:
- Standard dose or slightly higher
- 20-40mg typical
- Sublingual for faster onset
- Can redose

---

## What CBD Won't Fix

### Limitations

Be realistic:
- Won't cure hangover
- Doesn't reverse dehydration
- Not a miracle cure
- One part of recovery

### What You Still Need

Essential:
- Water (hydration is key)
- Electrolytes
- Rest
- Time

---

## Better Hangover Strategy

### Full Approach

Combine:
- Water before bed, upon waking
- Electrolytes (sports drinks, coconut water)
- Light food when possible
- CBD for discomfort
- Rest

### Prevention

Best strategy:
- Moderate drinking
- Water between drinks
- Food while drinking
- Pace yourself

---

## CBD and Alcohol Interaction

### Note

Consideration:
- Both may have relaxing effects
- CBD before drinking may intensify relaxation
- Research on interaction limited
- Use caution

### Not a Pass to Drink More

Important:
- CBD doesn't protect liver
- Doesn't reduce alcohol toxicity
- Not an excuse for excess
- Still need responsible drinking

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD may help** some hangover symptoms
2. **Not a hangover cure** on its own
3. **Hydration remains essential**
4. **Part of recovery** strategy, not replacement
5. **Prevention** is still the best approach
