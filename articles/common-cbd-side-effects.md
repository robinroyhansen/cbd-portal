# Common CBD Side Effects: What to Expect

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD is generally well-tolerated, but common side effects include fatigue, digestive changes, dry mouth, and appetite changes. These effects are typically mild and dose-dependent, often resolving as your body adjusts or with dose reduction.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Most common: fatigue, digestive issues, dry mouth
- Side effects are usually mild and temporary
- Higher doses increase likelihood of effects
- Individual responses vary significantly
- Most people tolerate CBD well

---

## Most Common Side Effects

### Fatigue/Drowsiness

- Most frequently reported side effect
- More common at higher doses
- May be beneficial for sleep-focused use
- Usually decreases with continued use

### Digestive Changes

- Diarrhoea or stomach upset
- More common with high doses
- May relate to carrier oil
- Usually temporary

### Dry Mouth

- CBD may affect saliva production
- Common with many cannabinoids
- Easily managed with hydration

### Appetite Changes

- Can increase or decrease appetite
- Individual variation significant
- Usually mild effect

### Dizziness (Rare but Possible)

- May relate to blood pressure effects
- More common at high doses
- Usually temporary

---

## Managing Side Effects

### Strategies

1. **Reduce dose** — Often most effective
2. **Change timing** — Take at bedtime if causing fatigue
3. **Take with food** — May reduce digestive effects
4. **Stay hydrated** — Helps with dry mouth
5. **Be patient** — Effects often decrease with time

---

## When to Seek Medical Attention

Contact a healthcare provider if:
- Severe or persistent side effects
- Symptoms affecting daily function
- Unexpected or unusual reactions
- Taking medications (potential interactions)

---

## My Take

Side effects from CBD are generally mild compared to many pharmaceuticals. Most people tolerate it well, and when side effects occur, they're usually manageable with dose adjustment. The fatigue some experience can actually be beneficial if using CBD for sleep.
