By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Summary

CBD and chiropractic care both address musculoskeletal issues through different mechanisms. Combining them may enhance results for back pain, joint issues, and overall mobility—an increasingly common integrative approach.

---

## Understanding the Combination

### How Chiropractic Works

Mechanism:
- Spinal adjustments
- Joint manipulation
- Nerve interference reduction
- Structural alignment

### How CBD Helps

Complementary effects:
- Reduces inflammation
- Relaxes muscles
- Decreases pain perception
- Supports healing

### Why Combine

Potential benefits:
- Different mechanisms work together
- CBD may enhance adjustment benefits
- Longer-lasting relief
- Holistic approach

## My Take

The combination of CBD and chiropractic care makes practical sense. Chiropractic addresses structural issues, while CBD handles inflammation and muscle tension. Many chiropractors now incorporate CBD into their practice because they see better patient outcomes.

If you're using chiropractic care, adding CBD may extend the benefits of your adjustments and reduce post-treatment soreness. Discuss it with your chiropractor—they likely have experience with this combination.
