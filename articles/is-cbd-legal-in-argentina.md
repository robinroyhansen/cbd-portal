# Is CBD Legal in Argentina? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal for medical use in Argentina** with proper authorization. Argentina was one of the first South American countries to establish a medical cannabis framework. Consumer CBD products exist in a gray area, with some availability but unclear regulation.

---

## Legal Framework

### Medical Cannabis Law

Argentina's approach:
- Medical cannabis legal since 2017
- CBD products for medical use
- Registration system exists
- Expanded in 2020

### Consumer Products

Current status:
- Medical CBD regulated
- Consumer market developing
- Some products available
- Gray area exists

---

## Access Options

### Medical Authorization

For patients:
- Register with REPROCANN
- Medical prescription required
- Self-cultivation permitted
- Pharmacy products expanding

### Consumer Products

Reality:
- Some CBD products sold
- Health stores, pharmacies
- Quality varies
- Not fully regulated

---

## For Tourists

### Visiting Argentina

Considerations:
- Don't bring CBD into Argentina
- Local products may be available
- Quality uncertain
- Stick to known brands if purchasing

## Key Takeaways

## My Take

Argentina has been relatively progressive on medical cannabis in South America. For visitors, don't bring CBD but you may find products locally in Buenos Aires and other cities. Quality verification is advisable.
