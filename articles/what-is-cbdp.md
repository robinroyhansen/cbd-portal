# What is CBDP (Cannabidiphorol)?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBDP (Cannabidiphorol) is a naturally occurring cannabinoid discovered alongside THCP in 2019. Like CBD, CBDP is non-psychoactive. It has a longer side chain than regular CBD (7 carbons vs 5), which may affect how it interacts with the body. Research on CBDP is extremely limited.

---

## What is CBDP?

CBDP is the cannabidiol analog discovered by the same Italian research team that found THCP. It's structurally similar to CBD but with a longer alkyl side chain.

**Key characteristics:**
- Non-psychoactive (like CBD)
- Naturally occurring in trace amounts
- Discovered in 2019
- Longer side chain than CBD
- Minimal research available

---

## CBDP vs CBD

| Property | CBDP | CBD |
|----------|------|-----|
| Side chain | 7 carbons | 5 carbons |
| Psychoactive | No | No |
| Natural abundance | Trace | Significant |
| Research | Minimal | Extensive |
| Products available | Rare | Common |

---

## Potential Significance

The discovery of CBDP suggests:
- More cannabinoid variations exist than previously known
- Side chain length affects receptor interactions
- Cannabis chemistry is more complex than thought

However, whether the longer chain makes CBDP more effective than CBD is unknown.

---

## Current Research Status

CBDP research is essentially:
- Initial identification and characterization
- Basic receptor binding studies (very limited)
- No clinical trials
- No established therapeutic applications

---

## CBDP in Products

CBDP is rarely found in commercial products because:
- It occurs in very small amounts naturally
- Synthesis is not widely developed
- No established market demand
- CBD itself is already available and researched

---

## My Take

CBDP is scientifically interesting but practically irrelevant for consumers right now. CBD has extensive research supporting its safety and potential benefits. Until CBDP is actually studied, there's no reason to seek it out over regular CBD.
