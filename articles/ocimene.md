# Ocimene: The Sweet, Herbaceous Defence Terpene

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Ocimene is a sweet, herbaceous terpene found in many plants including cannabis, mint, parsley, and orchids. Known for its pleasant aroma and potential antifungal, antibacterial, and anti-inflammatory properties, ocimene contributes to the complex terpene profiles of many CBD products.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Sweet, woody, herbaceous aroma with citrus undertones
- Strong antifungal and antibacterial properties in research
- May have anti-inflammatory and decongestant effects
- Common in plants as a defence mechanism
- Found in mint, parsley, orchids, and cannabis

---

## What Is Ocimene?

Ocimene is a monoterpene that exists in three forms (isomers). It's notable for being part of plants' natural defence systems—released to deter pests and attract pest-predators.

Natural sources include:
- **Mint** — Contributing to fresh aroma
- **Parsley** — Part of herb's scent
- **Orchids** — Attracts pollinators
- **Basil** — Component of aroma
- **Kumquats** — Citrus contribution
- **Cannabis** — Various strains

## Ocimene's Effects and Benefits

### Antimicrobial Properties

Research shows:
- Strong antifungal activity
- Antibacterial effects against various strains
- Potential applications in natural preservation
- May support immune function

### Anti-Inflammatory Potential

Studies suggest:
- May reduce inflammatory responses
- Potential decongestant properties
- Traditional use for respiratory support

### Possible Uplifting Effects

Anecdotally associated with:
- Energising, uplifting experiences
- Mental clarity
- Daytime-appropriate effects

## Ocimene in CBD Products

Less common as a dominant terpene but found in:
- Full-spectrum products with diverse profiles
- Certain sativa-leaning strains
- Products with complex, herbal aromas

Notable strains:
- **Golden Goat** — Ocimene presence
- **Strawberry Cough** — Contains ocimene
- **Clementine** — Part of citrus profile

## My Take

Ocimene is a behind-the-scenes terpene that adds complexity without dominating. Its antimicrobial research is genuinely interesting, and its fresh, herbaceous quality brightens many terpene profiles. While you won't usually seek out high-ocimene products specifically, appreciating its contribution helps you understand why certain CBD products feel particularly fresh and clean.
