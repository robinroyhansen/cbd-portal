# CBD During Detox: Support for Withdrawal and Recovery

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD has gained interest as a potential support during detox and recovery from various substances. Research is early but suggests CBD may help with certain aspects of withdrawal and craving management. Professional medical supervision remains essential during detox.

---

## Important Disclaimer

**Medical supervision required:**
- Detox can be dangerous
- Some withdrawals are life-threatening
- Always work with professionals
- CBD doesn't replace treatment

- Supports emotional state

### Sleep Support
