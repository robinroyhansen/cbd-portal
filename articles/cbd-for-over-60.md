# CBD for Over 60: A Complete Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD can be a valuable wellness option for adults over 60, but requires careful consideration of age-related factors including slower metabolism, medication interactions, and increased sensitivity. Seniors benefit from starting with lower doses than younger adults and should always consult healthcare providers, especially given common polypharmacy. Many over-60s use CBD successfully for sleep, discomfort, and general wellness.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Start with lower doses (5-10mg)
- Medication interactions are key concern
- Slower metabolism means stronger effects
- Healthcare provider consultation essential
- Many seniors benefit from CBD

### Age-Related Changes

**Physical Changes:**
- Slower drug metabolism
- Reduced liver function
- Different body composition
- Increased sensitivity to many substances

**Practical Impact:**
Standard adult dosing may be too high for seniors.

## Starting CBD Over 60

### Recommended Approach

**Step 1: Consult Healthcare Provider**
- Essential first step
- Discuss all medications
- Get professional input
- Address concerns

**Step 2: Choose Appropriate Product**
- Lower-potency products
- Easy-to-dose formats
- Quality-tested
- Reputable brand

**Step 3: Start Very Low**
- 5-10mg to start (half normal adult dose)
- Take in evening initially
- One dose per day to begin

**Step 4: Increase Slowly**
- Wait at least a week before increasing
- Increase by 5mg at a time
- Monitor carefully
- Find minimum effective dose

## Dosing Guidelines

### Recommended Starting Doses

| Age | Starting Dose | Increase By |
|-----|---------------|-------------|
| 60-70 | 5-10mg | 5mg weekly |
| 70-80 | 5mg | 5mg every 1-2 weeks |
| 80+ | 5mg | Very slowly |

### Why Lower Doses

- Slower metabolism
- Increased sensitivity
- Medication interactions
- Reduced clearance
- Effects last longer

## Medication Interactions

### Major Concern for This Age Group

**Common Over-60 Medications:**
- Blood thinners (warfarin)
- Blood pressure medications
- Heart medications
- Diabetes drugs
- Pain medications
- Sleep aids
- Antidepressants

### Blood Thinner Warning

**Warfarin Especially:**
- CBD increases warfarin levels
- Bleeding risk increases
- Requires INR monitoring
- Must discuss with doctor

### General Interaction Risk

If you take multiple medications, interaction risk is higher. Always consult healthcare provider.

## Safety Considerations

### Fall Risk

**CBD May Cause:**
- Dizziness
- Drowsiness
- Blood pressure changes

**Precautions:**
- Take when safely at home initially
- Rise slowly from sitting
- Avoid nighttime bathroom trips right after dosing
- Tell family you're starting CBD

### Cognitive Effects

**Watch For:**
- Any confusion
- Memory changes
- Mental fog
- Unusual behaviour

**If Noticed:**
Stop CBD and consult healthcare provider.

### Blood Pressure

**Considerations:**
- CBD may lower blood pressure slightly
- Combined with BP medications, effect amplified
- Monitor blood pressure
- Report dizziness to doctor
