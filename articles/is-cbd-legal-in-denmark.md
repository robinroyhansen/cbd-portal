# Is CBD Legal in Denmark? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Denmark** when derived from EU-approved hemp with less than 0.2% THC. Denmark has a regulatory framework that permits CBD products, though with some restrictions on CBD flowers and Novel Food considerations. The market is developing, with products available in shops and online.

---

## Legal Framework

### Danish CBD Regulations

Denmark follows EU hemp guidelines with national implementation:
- Hemp cultivation from EU-approved varieties
- THC limit: 0.2% (standard EU limit)
- CBD products permitted with compliance
- Novel Food regulations apply
- Some restrictions on flowers/plant material

### The Medicine Question

In Denmark, CBD has faced classification questions:
- Some products considered medicines (restricted)
- Others permitted as food/supplements
- Classification depends on presentation/claims
- Regulatory clarity has improved

---

## What's Legal

### Clearly Legal

- CBD oils and extracts with <0.2% THC
- CBD cosmetics
- Hemp food products
- Properly marketed supplements

### Restricted/Complex

- CBD flowers (restrictions apply)
- Products with health claims
- High-concentration products (may face scrutiny)

### Prohibited

- THC above 0.2%
- Medical claims without authorization
- Products classified as medicines without approval
