By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Myanmar**. The country maintains strict prohibition on all cannabis products with severe penalties. The political situation adds additional complexity. Do not bring CBD to Myanmar.

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Myanmar's stance:
- All cannabis strictly prohibited
- Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Law
- Severe penalties including death penalty for trafficking
- No CBD distinction

### Current Situation
