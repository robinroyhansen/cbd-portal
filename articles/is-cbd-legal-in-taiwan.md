# Is CBD Legal in Taiwan? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Taiwan**. Taiwan classifies CBD as a controlled substance under its Narcotics Hazard Prevention Act. All CBD products are prohibited regardless of THC content. **Do not bring CBD to Taiwan.**

---

## Taiwan's Drug Laws

### Legal Status

Taiwan's position:
- CBD classified as Category 2 narcotic
- All cannabinoids controlled
- No hemp-derived exemption
- Strict enforcement

---

## Penalties

Consequences:
- Possession: Up to 3 years prison
- Import/trafficking: More severe
- Foreigners face deportation
- No exceptions for CBD

---

## For Travelers

### Critical Warning

If visiting Taiwan:
- Do NOT bring any CBD products
- Not available to purchase
- Strict customs screening
- Serious consequences
