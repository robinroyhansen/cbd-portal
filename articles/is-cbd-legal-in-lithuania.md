# Is CBD Legal in Lithuania? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Lithuania** when derived from EU-approved industrial hemp containing less than 0.2% THC. Lithuania has a developing CBD market with products available in Vilnius and Kaunas, though the market is smaller than in Western Europe.

---

## Legal Framework

### Lithuanian CBD Regulations

Lithuania's approach:
- EU member since 2004
- Industrial hemp cultivation legal
- THC limit: 0.2% (EU standard)
- CBD products permitted commercially
- Novel Food regulations apply
- Growing market awareness

### Classification

CBD in Lithuania:
- Not a controlled substance (when compliant)
- Cannot make medical claims
- Food/supplement regulations apply
- Cosmetics permitted

---

## What's Legal

### Clearly Legal

- CBD oils with <0.2% THC
- CBD cosmetics and topicals
- Hemp food products
- CBD capsules and supplements

### Complex/Restricted

- CBD flowers (grey area)
- Products with health claims

### Prohibited

- THC above 0.2%
- Medical claims without authorization
- Sale to minors
