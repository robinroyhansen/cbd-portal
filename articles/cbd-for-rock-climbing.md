# CBD for Rock Climbing: Recovery and Performance Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Rock climbing demands finger strength, upper body endurance, and mental focus. CBD may help climbers with finger and forearm recovery, anxiety management on difficult routes, and sleep after hard climbing days.

---

## Why Climbers Use CBD

### Physical Demands

Primary concerns:
- Finger and hand stress
- Forearm pump and fatigue
- Shoulder and back strain
- Skin healing from holds

### Mental Demands

Climbing-specific:
- Fear and anxiety management
- Focus on routes/problems
- Lead climbing nerves
- Mental fatigue on long days

## How CBD Helps Climbers

### Finger and Forearm Recovery

Critical for climbers:
- Tendons and pulleys stressed
- Forearm pump is limiting
- CBD's anti-inflammatory effects
- May support recovery

### Anxiety Management

On difficult routes:
- Lead climbing fear
- Exposure and heights
- Commitment moves
- CBD's calming effects

### Sleep and Recovery

## Common Climbing Issues

### Finger Injuries

Pulleys and tendons:
- A2 pulley strains common
- Tendon stress
- CBD may help inflammation
- Not a substitute for rest

### Forearm Pump

Limiting factor:
- Blood flow restricted
- Grip strength fails
- CBD won't prevent pump
- Recovery support only

### Shoulder Stress

From reaching:
- Rotator cuff demands
- Lock-off positions
- Topical application
- Prehab more important

### Skin Healing

From holds:
- Flappers and splits
- Abrasion
- CBD balm may help
- Supports skin health
