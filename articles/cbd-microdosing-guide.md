# CBD Microdosing: The Low-Dose Approach

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Microdosing CBD means taking very small, sub-therapeutic doses throughout the day. Some people prefer this approach for subtle, consistent support without stronger effects. This guide explains when and how to microdose CBD.

---

## What Is CBD Microdosing?

### Definition

Microdosing:
- Taking very small doses
- Multiple times throughout day
- Below "standard" therapeutic dose
- Subtle, ongoing effects

### Typical Microdoses

Amounts:
- 2-10mg per dose
- 2-4 times daily
- Total daily: 10-30mg
- Compared to standard 25-50mg+

---

## Why Microdose?

### Potential Benefits

Reasons to try:
- Consistent subtle support
- Avoid any drowsiness
- Maintain alertness
- Sensitive to substances
- Cost-effective

### Who Might Benefit

Good candidates:
- Highly sensitive individuals
- Want daytime function
- Mild symptoms only
- Budget-conscious
- Trying CBD first time

---

## How Microdosing Works

### Consistent Levels

Theory:
- Maintain steady CBD levels
- Avoid peaks and valleys
- Ongoing endocannabinoid support
- Subtle regulation

### Biphasic Response

CBD characteristic:
- Low doses may be stimulating
- High doses more sedating
- Microdosing stays in lower range
- Different effect profile

---

## How to Microdose CBD

### Starting Protocol

Approach:
- Begin with 2-5mg per dose
- Take 2-3 times daily
- Maintain for 1-2 weeks
- Evaluate effects

### Timing

Schedule:
- Morning dose
- Early afternoon dose
- Evening dose (optional or higher)
- Consistent timing

### Product Selection

Best for microdosing:
- Tinctures (precise dosing)
- Low-dose gummies
- CBD water/beverages
- Avoid high-dose products

---

## Calculating Microdoses

### With Tinctures

Math:
- Know mg per dropper
- 1000mg/30ml = ~33mg per full dropper
- Quarter dropper = ~8mg
- Adjust based on your product

### With Gummies

Options:
- Cut larger gummies
- Buy low-dose (5-10mg) gummies
- Consistent portions
- Less precision than tincture

---

## When Microdosing Makes Sense

### Good Fit

Consider if:
- Mild symptoms
- Sensitivity to substances
- Need to stay sharp
- Budget limited
- Exploring CBD

### Not Ideal

May not work if:
- Moderate-severe symptoms
- Need stronger relief
- Have tried standard dosing successfully
- Want faster results

---

## Comparing Approaches

### Microdosing vs Standard Dosing

| Factor | Microdosing | Standard |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| Dose | 2-10mg | 25-50mg+ |
| Frequency | Multiple daily | 1-2x daily |
| Effects | Subtle | More noticeable |
| Alertness | Maintained | May cause drowsiness |
| Cost | Lower per day | Higher per day |

---

## Adjusting Your Approach

### If Not Enough

Try:
- Increase dose slightly
- Add another daily dose
- Consider standard dosing
- Give more time

### If Too Much

Adjust:
- Lower each dose
- Reduce frequency
- You're finding your threshold
- Sensitivity identified

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Very small doses** (2-10mg) multiple times daily
2. **Subtle effects** maintained throughout day
3. **Good for sensitive** individuals
4. **May not be enough** for significant symptoms
5. **Tinctures best** for precise dosing
