# CBD for Dental Anxiety: Managing Fear of the Dentist

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Dental anxiety prevents many people from getting needed dental care. CBD's anxiety-reducing properties may help manage fear of the dentist, allowing you to attend appointments more comfortably.

---

## Understanding Dental Anxiety

### How Common It Is

Reality:
- Affects up to 20% of people
- Can be mild nervousness to severe phobia
- Leads to avoided dental care
- Worse outcomes from delay

### What Triggers It

Common fears:
- Pain anticipation
- Loss of control
- Past negative experiences
- Sounds and smells
- Needles/injections

---

## How CBD May Help

### Anxiety Reduction

Primary mechanism:
- CBD's established anxiolytic effects
- Reduces anticipatory anxiety
- Calms physical symptoms
- Allows appointment attendance

### Physical Symptoms

May help:
- Racing heart
- Sweating
- Muscle tension
- Nervous stomach

---

## Using CBD for Dental Appointments

### Timing

Approach:
- Take 45-60 minutes before appointment
- Allow effects to develop
- Don't wait until last minute
- Effects through procedure

### Dosing

For dental anxiety:
- Moderate dose (20-30mg)
- Enough for meaningful effect
- Test beforehand if possible
- Know your response

### Tell Your Dentist

Important:
- Inform dentist about CBD use
- Part of health disclosure
- They can adjust approach
- Won't affect your care negatively

---

## What CBD Won't Do

### Limitations

Be realistic:
- Won't eliminate all fear
- Not sedation
- Doesn't numb pain
- Still may need other interventions

### Severe Dental Phobia

When more is needed:
- Consider dental sedation
- Discuss with dentist
- Psychological help may benefit
- CBD alone may be insufficient

---

## Complementary Strategies

### With CBD

Also helpful:
- Communicate with dental staff
- Agree on signals to pause
- Listen to music/podcasts
- Deep breathing exercises
- Choose understanding dentist

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD may help** dental anxiety
2. **Take before** appointments (45-60 min)
3. **Tell your dentist** about CBD use
4. **Test beforehand** if possible
5. **May need more** for severe phobia
