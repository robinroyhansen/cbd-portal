## Performance Anxiety

### Stage Fright

Common experience:
- Pre-show nerves
- Trembling hands
- Racing thoughts
- Physical tension

### How CBD May Help

Benefits:
- Anxiolytic effects
- Calms physical symptoms
- Maintains alertness
- Won't affect playing ability

### Before Performances

Timing:
- 30-60 minutes before show
- Low-moderate dose (15-25mg)
- Test beforehand in rehearsals
- Know your response

## Physical Applications

### Repetitive Strain

Musicians' issues:
- Hand and wrist strain
- Shoulder tension
- Back pain from posture
- Embouchure strain (wind players)

### CBD Approach

Application:
- Topical to affected areas
- Oral for systemic support
- Part of injury prevention
- Doesn't replace proper technique

### Instrument-Specific

Common issues:
- Guitarists: Wrist, fingers
- Drummers: Wrists, shoulders, back
- Pianists: Hands, forearms
- Wind players: Embouchure, breathing
