# The Future of CBD Research: What's Coming

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD research is accelerating, with more clinical trials, better methodologies, and expanding focus areas. Future directions include larger human trials, personalised dosing, novel delivery methods, combination therapies, and investigations into new therapeutic applications beyond currently studied conditions.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Research pace accelerating significantly
- Larger, longer human trials underway
- Personalised medicine approaches emerging
- New therapeutic areas being explored
- Better products and delivery developing
