# CBD for Tennis: Recovery and Performance Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Summary

Tennis combines explosive movements, repetitive arm motion, and mental pressure over potentially long matches. CBD may help tennis players with recovery, inflammation management, and the mental demands of competition.

---

## Why Tennis Players Use CBD

### Physical Demands

Primary concerns:
- Shoulder and arm stress
- Knee and ankle impact
- Core and back tension
- Match recovery

### Mental Demands

Tennis-specific:
- Match pressure and nerves
- Focus during long points
- Handling momentum shifts
- Between-point composure

---

## How CBD Helps Tennis Players

### Arm and Shoulder Recovery

From serving and groundstrokes:
- High-velocity repetitive motion
- Rotator cuff stress
- CBD's anti-inflammatory effects
- Supports recovery between sessions

### Lower Body Impact

Movement demands:
- Lateral movement stress
- Stop-start impact on knees
- Ankle stability work
- Recovery from court surface

### Mental Game

Competition support:
- Pre-match anxiety
- Staying calm in pressure points
- Focus maintenance
- Handling match stress

## Using CBD for Tennis

### Practice Days

Approach:
- Post-practice recovery
- Moderate doses (25-40mg)
- Topical for arm/shoulder
- Evening for sleep

### Match Days

Strategy:
- Small dose 1-2 hours before
- Focus on anxiety management
- Don't impair performance
- Post-match recovery dose

### Tournament Play

Multi-day events:
- Consistent daily use
- Focus on recovery
- Sleep optimization
- Manage inflammation accumulation

## Heat and Hydration

### Playing in Heat

Considerations:
- Stay well hydrated
- CBD doesn't replace fluids
- May affect heat response
- Be cautious in extreme heat

### Recovery in Heat

Approach:
- Hydration first
- CBD for recovery
- Not during active play
- Post-match focus
