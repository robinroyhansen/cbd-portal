# CBD for Family Gatherings: Navigating Holiday and Event Stress

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Family gatherings can be wonderful but also stressful. Complex dynamics, old patterns, and high expectations create unique anxiety. CBD may help you stay calm, patient, and emotionally regulated during these often-challenging events.

---

## Why Family Events Are Stressful

### Unique Stressors

Contributing factors:
- Long-standing dynamics
- Old roles and expectations
- Unresolved issues
- Competing personalities
- Obligation vs. desire

### Common Triggers

What happens:
- Falling into old patterns
- Unsolicited advice/criticism
- Comparison to siblings/cousins
- Political disagreements
- Boundary violations

---

## How CBD May Help

### Emotional Regulation

Key benefit:
- Stay calmer when triggered
- Better impulse control
- Less reactive responses
- Maintain composure
- Patience preservation

### Stress Management

Support for:
- Anxiety about attending
- During-event tension
- Physical stress symptoms
- Post-event processing

---

## Before the Gathering

### Preparation Protocol

Approach:
- Start CBD day before if anxious
- Morning dose day of
- Pre-event dose 1-2 hours before
- Set intentions for calm

### Mental Preparation

Alongside CBD:
- Set realistic expectations
- Plan responses to triggers
- Identify exit strategies
- Prepare conversation topics

---

## During the Event

### Maintaining Calm

Strategies:
- CBD supporting background calm
- Take breaks when needed
- Deep breathing
- Step outside periodically

### When Triggered

Response:
- CBD helps with reactive impulses
- Pause before responding
- Choose not to engage
- Walk away if needed

### Dosing During Event

Considerations:
- Long events may need redosing
- Be cautious with alcohol
- Stay functional
- Have product accessible

## My Take

Family gatherings are complicated precisely because we care. The stress comes from wanting connection while navigating difficult dynamics. CBD can help you stay regulated enough to respond rather than react, potentially preventing escalation you'd regret.

But CBD isn't a magic solution for family dysfunction. It helps you manage your own responses; it doesn't change anyone else. Sometimes the best approach is accepting that some gatherings will be difficult and having tools (like CBD) to survive them with your wellbeing intact.
