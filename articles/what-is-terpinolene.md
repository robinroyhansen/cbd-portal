# What is Terpinolene?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

Terpinolene is a [terpene](/knowledge/what-are-terpenes) with a complex, multidimensional aroma—floral, herbal, piney, and slightly citrusy all at once. Though rarely the dominant terpene in cannabis, when it does appear prominently, it's associated with uplifting, creative, and mildly sedating effects. Research suggests antioxidant, antibacterial, and potential anticancer properties.

---

## Terpinolene Profile

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Chemical formula** | C₁₀H₁₆ |
| **Aroma** | Complex—floral, herbal, piney, citrus, sweet |
| **Boiling point** | 186°C (367°F) |
| **Prevalence in cannabis** | Less common as dominant terpene |
| **Also found in** | Lilacs, tea tree, nutmeg, cumin, apples |

---

## The Least Common "Common" Terpene

Terpinolene is found in many cannabis strains but rarely dominates. When it does, those strains tend to be memorable:

- **Jack Herer** (and derivatives)
- **Dutch Treat**
- **Ghost Train Haze**
- **XJ-13**

These strains are often described as energizing and creative.

---

## Potential Effects and Benefits

Research on terpinolene suggests:

**Antioxidant**
- Strong antioxidant activity documented
- May protect cells from damage
- Potential health-protective effects

**Sedative (Interestingly)**
- Despite "uplifting" strain associations
- Animal studies show sedative effects
- May contribute to relaxation at certain doses

**Antibacterial and Antifungal**
- Active against various microbes
- Used in soaps and cleansers
- Traditional antimicrobial use

**Anticancer (Preclinical)**
- Early research shows promise
- In-vitro studies on cancer cells
- Much more research needed

**Insect Repellent**
- Natural pest deterrent
- Used in natural insect repellents
- Part of plant defense system

---

## The Paradox: Uplifting Yet Sedative?

Terpinolene presents an interesting contradiction:
- Associated with "uplifting" strains
- Yet shows sedative effects in research

**Possible explanations:**
- Dose-dependent effects (stimulating at low doses, sedating at high)
- Interaction with other terpenes and cannabinoids
- Individual variation in response
- Multiple mechanisms of action

This complexity is typical of terpenes and underscores the importance of the entourage effect.

## Terpinolene in CBD Products

Products featuring terpinolene may be marketed as:
- Creative or uplifting formulas
- Unique terpene profiles
- Specialty or artisanal products
- Complex aromatic blends

The multifaceted aroma makes terpinolene-rich products distinctive.

## Research Status

Terpinolene research includes:
- Antioxidant activity studies
- Sedative effects in animal models
- Antimicrobial testing
- Early cancer research (in-vitro)

Human clinical studies are limited, as with most terpenes.

## My Take

Terpinolene-dominant products have a distinctive character that's hard to describe—simultaneously fresh, herbal, and floral. The strains famous for high terpinolene (like Jack Herer) have dedicated followings for a reason. While not the most common terpene profile, when you find a CBD product with significant terpinolene, it's worth trying for the unique experience it offers.
