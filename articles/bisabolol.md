## Quick Summary

Alpha-bisabolol is a gentle, floral terpene renowned for its skin-soothing and anti-inflammatory properties. Found in chamomile and cannabis, bisabolol is particularly valued in CBD topicals and skincare products for its ability to reduce irritation and enhance absorption of other compounds.

## What Is Bisabolol?

Alpha-bisabolol (also called levomenol) is a monocyclic sesquiterpene alcohol. It's the primary active compound responsible for chamomile's renowned calming and skin-soothing properties.

Natural sources:
- **German chamomile** — Primary commercial source
- **Candeia tree** — Brazilian source
- **Cannabis** — Present in various strains
