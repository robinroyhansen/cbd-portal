## Quick Summary

Content creation demands creativity, consistency, and the ability to work under pressure. CBD may help content creators manage creative anxiety, support sustained work sessions, and maintain the mental clarity needed for quality content.

### Mental Load

Cognitive demands:

## When to Use

### During Ideation

For creative thinking:
- Moderate doses
- Quiet mental noise
- Support free association
- Reduce perfectionism

### Production Work

For creating content:
- Lower doses preserve focus
- Support sustained effort
- Reduce technical anxiety
- Maintain quality

### Rest Days

For recovery:
- Higher doses for relaxation
- Support actual rest
- Prevent burnout
- Recharge creativity

---

## Dosing for Creators

### Creative Work

Recommended:
- 10-20mg for light calming
- Enough to reduce anxiety
- Not so much it slows thinking
- Test and adjust

### Recovery Focus

Higher doses:
- 25-40mg for relaxation
- When creativity isn't needed
- Support genuine rest
- Evening use

---

## Practical Tips

### Protect Creativity

CBD should:
- Support, not replace, creative process
- Reduce barriers, not create dependence
- Be one tool among many
- Complement good habits

### Track Effects

Monitor:
- Creative output quality
- Energy levels
- Focus duration
- Overall wellbeing

## Key Takeaways

1. **Lower doses** during creative work
2. **Higher doses** for rest and recovery
3. **Consistency** helps manage chronic stress
4. **One tool** among many for creator wellbeing
5. **Track effects** to optimize your approach
