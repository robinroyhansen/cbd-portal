## Quick Summary

Photography demands creative vision, physical stamina, and the ability to work under time pressure. CBD may help photographers manage shoot-day stress, recover from physically demanding sessions, and maintain the calm focus needed for capturing decisive moments.

---

## Photography Challenges

### Job Demands

What photography requires:
- Creative vision
- Technical precision
- Client management
- Physical endurance
- Time-sensitive work

### Physical Stressors

Common issues:
- Long hours on feet
- Heavy equipment carrying
- Awkward positions
- Repetitive strain
- Back and neck tension

Potential benefits:

Post-shoot support:
- Reduces muscle soreness
- Supports joint recovery

### Creative Focus

Mental benefits:

## Timing for Photographers

### Before Shoots

Pre-shoot approach:
- 30-60 minutes before
- Moderate calming dose
- Don't impair alertness
- Support confident presence

### During Long Sessions

Extended shoots:
- May redose if needed
- Stay hydrated
- Use topicals for soreness
- Maintain energy balance

### Post-Session Recovery

After shooting:
- Higher oral doses
- Topical application to sore areas
- Support sleep for early calls
- Aid muscle recovery

---

## Dosing Considerations

### For Work

During shoots:
- 10-20mg oral for light calming
- Topicals as needed for soreness
- Preserve alertness
- Test beforehand

### For Recovery

Post-work:
- 25-50mg for deeper relaxation
- Topicals on affected areas
- Support sleep if needed
- No alertness concerns

---

## Practical Tips

### Event Photography

For high-pressure events:
- Test during less critical work
- Moderate doses only
- Maintain reaction time
- Stay sharp for moments

### Studio Work

Controlled environment:
- More flexibility with timing
- Can adjust as needed
- Support sustained focus
- Manage client interaction stress

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Moderate doses** during active work
2. **Higher doses** for recovery periods
3. **Topicals** for physical strain
4. **Test first** before important shoots
5. **Consistency** helps overall stress
