# Is CBD Legal in Kenya? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Kenya**. The country prohibits all cannabis-derived products under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (Control) Act. There is no distinction between CBD and THC under Kenyan law.

---

## Legal Framework

### Current Status

Kenya's position:
- All cannabis prohibited
- CBD not exempted
- No medical cannabis
- Strict enforcement

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Imprisonment
- Fines
- Criminal record
- Possible deportation for foreigners

---

## For Travelers

### Clear Warning

Advice:
- Do not bring CBD to Kenya
- All products prohibited

- Exercise complete caution

### Safari and Tourism

Note:
