## Quick Summary

Both CBD and acupuncture work with the body's natural systems to promote balance and healing. Combining them may enhance benefits for pain, stress, and overall wellness—a growing trend in integrative health.

- Calms nervous system

Together:

- Different mechanisms, complementary effects
- May enhance each other
- Integrative approach

## How to Combine

### Before Acupuncture

Timing option:
- Take CBD 1-2 hours before
- May enhance relaxation
- Arrive calm
- Better session experience

### After Acupuncture

Alternative approach:
- Take CBD after session
- Extends relaxation
- Supports continued healing
- Evening dose for sleep

### Consistent CBD Use

Best approach:
- Regular daily CBD
- Acupuncture sessions as scheduled
- Ongoing combined support
- Not just session-specific

## My Take

Combining CBD and acupuncture makes intuitive sense—both work with the body's natural systems to promote balance. While research on the specific combination is limited, both therapies are individually well-supported, and practitioners report good results with the combination.

If you're already using one, trying the other alongside it is reasonable. Communication with your acupuncturist helps them tailor treatment knowing your complete picture.
