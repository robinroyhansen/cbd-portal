# CBD for Golfers: Focus, Calm, and Recovery on the Course

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Golf requires mental focus, steady nerves, and physical comfort over 4+ hours. CBD has become popular among golfers for managing first-tee jitters, supporting focus, and addressing the physical strains of the game. Many professionals now openly use CBD.

---

## Why Golfers Use CBD

### Golf's Demands

The game requires:
- Mental focus for hours
- Calm under pressure
- Physical comfort (walking, swinging)
- Recovery between rounds
- Nerve management on big shots

### How CBD May Help

Potential benefits:
- Reduces performance anxiety
- Supports focus
- Anti-inflammatory for joints
- Non-impairing during play

---

## On-Course Use

### Pre-Round

Many golfers:
- Take CBD 30-60 min before tee time
- Low dose (10-20mg)
- Helps with first-tee nerves
- Sets calm baseline

### During Round

Considerations:
- Effects last through round
- Re-dosing rarely needed
- Gummies convenient on course
- Won't impair swing or judgment

### For Tournaments

Competition use:
- Test routine in practice rounds
- Know exactly how it affects you
- Don't change routine on competition day
- CBD is not a banned substance
