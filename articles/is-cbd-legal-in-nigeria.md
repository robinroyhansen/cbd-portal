# Is CBD Legal in Nigeria? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Nigeria**. The National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA) classifies all cannabis products as controlled substances. Nigeria has strict anti-drug laws with severe penalties. Do not bring CBD to Nigeria.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Nigerian law:
- Cannabis completely prohibited
- No distinction for CBD
- No medical cannabis program
- NDLEA enforcement

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Imprisonment (up to life)
- Heavy fines
- Property seizure
- Serious criminal record
