# CBD for Shingles and Postherpetic Neuralgia

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Shingles causes a painful rash, and postherpetic neuralgia (PHN) is nerve pain that persists after shingles heals. CBD may help with both acute shingles pain and chronic PHN through anti-inflammatory and neuropathic pain effects.

---

## Understanding Shingles

### What It Is

Condition:
- Reactivation of varicella-zoster virus (chickenpox virus)
- Causes painful, blistering rash
- Usually one side of body
- Can occur at any age but more common with age

### Symptoms

Experience:
- Burning, tingling pain (often before rash)
- Painful rash with blisters
- Sensitivity in affected area
- Generally resolves in 2-4 weeks

---

## Postherpetic Neuralgia

### What It Is

PHN:
- Nerve pain persisting after shingles heals
- Can last months to years
- More common in older adults
- Can be severe and debilitating

### Why It Happens

Mechanism:
- Nerve damage from shingles
- Ongoing nerve dysfunction
- Pain signaling abnormalities
- Can be chronic

---

## How CBD May Help

### For Shingles Pain

During active shingles:
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- General pain modulation
- May support comfort
- Part of management

### For PHN

Chronic nerve pain:
- Neuropathic pain pathway
- Established research on CBD for nerve pain
- May complement other treatments
- Addresses chronic component

---

## Using CBD for Shingles/PHN

### Acute Shingles

Approach:
- Oral CBD for systemic support
- Topical ONLY after rash heals (not on open blisters)
- Along with antiviral treatment
- Standard pain management

### Postherpetic Neuralgia

For chronic pain:
- Oral CBD for nerve pain (25-50mg)
- Topical to affected area after healing
- Consistent daily use
- Part of comprehensive approach

### Important: Active Rash

Caution:
- Don't apply topicals to active rash/blisters
- Wait until skin heals
- Oral is appropriate during active shingles
- Focus on standard care during acute phase

---

## Standard Treatment

### Acute Shingles

Essential:
- Antiviral medication (early is key)
- Pain management
- Care of rash
- See doctor promptly

### PHN Treatment

Options:
- Prescription nerve pain medications
- Lidocaine patches
- Capsaicin cream
- Other interventions

### Prevention

Consider:
- Shingles vaccine (highly effective)
- Especially for those 50+
- Reduces PHN risk
- Talk to doctor

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD may help** with shingles and PHN pain
2. **Don't apply topicals** to active rash
3. **Get antiviral treatment** early for shingles
4. **PHN responds** to consistent CBD use potentially
5. **Standard treatments** remain important
