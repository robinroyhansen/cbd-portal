# Is CBD Legal in Uruguay? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Uruguay** as part of the country's pioneering cannabis legalization. Uruguay was the first country to fully legalize cannabis, and CBD products are available through regulated channels.

---

## Legal Framework

### Historic Legalization

Uruguay's position:
- First country to legalize cannabis (2013)
- CBD legal within this framework
- Regulated market established
- Government-controlled system

### How It Works

System:
- Pharmacies sell cannabis products
- Registration required for residents
- Medical and recreational pathways
- Quality-controlled products

### CBD Products

Availability:

## For Tourists

### Important Limitation

Key point:

- Tourists cannot buy from pharmacies
- Some products may be available otherwise
- Don't bring from abroad

### Practical Reality

Understanding:
