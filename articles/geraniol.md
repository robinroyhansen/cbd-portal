## Quick Summary

## What Is Geraniol?

Geraniol is a monoterpene alcohol with a distinctive sweet, floral, rose-like scent. It's extensively used in perfumery and cosmetics and is a primary component of rose and geranium essential oils.

Natural sources:
- **Rose oil** — Major component
- **Geranium** — Primary source for extraction
- **Palmarosa** — High geraniol content
- **Lemongrass** — Contains geraniol
- **Cannabis** — Present in various strains
