## Quick Summary

CrossFit's high-intensity, varied workouts create significant recovery demands. CBD may help CrossFitters recover faster, manage the inflammation from intense WODs, and optimize sleep for adaptation—all while remaining competition-legal.

## Using CBD for CrossFit

### Post-WOD Protocol

Approach:
- Take within 1-2 hours of training
- Combine with protein and nutrition
- Topical for specific problem areas
- Evening dose for sleep

### Dosing for CrossFit

Typical range:
- Regular WODs: 25-40mg
- Competition prep: 40-60mg
- Rest days: maintain or reduce
- Adjust based on training intensity

## My Take

CrossFit's constantly varied, high-intensity approach creates significant recovery demands. CBD fits well into a CrossFitter's recovery toolkit—it won't improve your Fran time, but it may help you recover better between sessions, allowing more consistent training.

For competitive athletes, product purity is essential. The CrossFit Games do test, so use verified THC-free products if you're competing at that level.
