# CBD for Panic Attacks: Can It Help?

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Panic attacks are sudden, intense episodes of fear with physical symptoms. While research on CBD for panic disorder is limited, general anxiety research suggests potential benefits. Understanding the nature of panic attacks helps set realistic expectations for CBD.

---

## Understanding Panic Attacks

### What They Are

Characteristics:
- Sudden onset (peak within minutes)
- Intense fear or discomfort
- Physical symptoms
- Often unexpected
- Can occur during sleep

### Physical Symptoms

Common experiences:
- Racing heart
- Sweating
- Trembling
- Shortness of breath
- Chest pain
- Dizziness
- Numbness/tingling

### Panic Disorder

When it's a disorder:
- Recurrent unexpected attacks
- Persistent worry about attacks
- Behavioral changes to avoid triggers
- Significant life impact

---

## CBD and Panic: The Evidence

### Current Research

What exists:
- General anxiety studies suggest benefit
- Limited panic-specific research
- Animal models show promise
- Mechanism research ongoing

### How CBD Might Help

Potential effects:
- May reduce baseline anxiety
- Could prevent build-up to panic
- Might ease anticipatory fear
- General calming effect

### Important Limitations

Be realistic:
- Won't stop panic attack in progress
- Not fast enough for acute episodes
- Best for prevention/management
- Not a replacement for treatment

---

## Using CBD for Panic

### Prevention Approach

Strategy:
- Daily consistent dosing
- Reduces baseline anxiety
- May decrease attack frequency
- Part of broader plan

### Not for Acute Episodes

Why daily works better:
- CBD takes time to work
- Can't stop attack once started
- Prevention more effective
- Build stable blood levels

### Dosing

General guidance:
- Start low (15-20mg)
- Build to effective dose
- Some need 30-50mg+
- Consistency matters

---

## What Actually Helps During Panic

### Immediate Techniques

When attack occurs:
- Slow breathing (4-7-8 technique)
- Grounding exercises
- Remember it will pass
- Don't fight it

### CBD's Role

Where CBD fits:
- Reduces baseline anxiety between attacks
- May lessen severity over time
- Supports other coping strategies
- Not a rescue medication

---

## Seeking Treatment

### When to Get Help

See a professional if:
- Attacks are frequent
- Significant life impact
- Avoiding situations due to fear
- Self-help isn't enough

### Evidence-Based Treatments

Options include:
- CBT for panic disorder
- Exposure therapy
- Medication when appropriate
- Combination approaches

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **CBD won't stop** an active panic attack
2. **Daily use may help** reduce frequency
3. **Prevention approach** most effective
4. **Works best** with proven treatments
5. **Seek professional help** for panic disorder
