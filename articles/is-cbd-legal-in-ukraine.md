# Is CBD Legal in Ukraine? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **technically illegal in Ukraine** as all cannabis derivatives are controlled. However, enforcement has been limited due to ongoing conflict, and there have been discussions about medical cannabis reform. The legal situation remains unclear for consumers.
