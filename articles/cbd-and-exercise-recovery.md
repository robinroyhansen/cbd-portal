# CBD and Exercise Recovery: Post-Workout Support

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Athletes and fitness enthusiasts increasingly use CBD for exercise recovery. CBD's anti-inflammatory properties and potential sleep benefits may support the recovery process. Both oral and topical products have applications for post-workout use.

---

## Why CBD for Recovery

### Recovery Needs

After exercise:
- Muscle inflammation
- Micro-damage repair
- Energy restoration
- Sleep for adaptation
- Reduced soreness

### How CBD May Help

Potential benefits:
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Pain modulation
- Sleep improvement
- Non-impairing recovery support

---

## Post-Workout CBD Use

### Timing

When to use:
- Immediately post-workout
- Evening after training day
- Before bed for sleep recovery
- Part of cool-down routine

### Product Options

**Oral CBD:**
- Systemic anti-inflammatory
- Sleep support
- Overall recovery

**Topical CBD:**
- Target sore muscles
- Specific areas of strain
- Direct application

### Combined Approach

Many athletes:
- Topical to sore areas post-workout
- Oral for systemic support and sleep
- Addresses recovery from multiple angles

---

## Dosing for Athletes

### Suggested Amounts

Post-workout:
- Oral: 20-40mg
- Topical: Apply generously
- Higher demands = may need more
- Individual variation

### Heavy Training

During intense blocks:
- Higher doses may help
- Track recovery quality
- Adjust accordingly

### Complete Recovery Protocol

Combine CBD with:
- Proper nutrition

- Adequate sleep
- Active recovery
- Rest days

### CBD Complements, Doesn't Replace

Remember:
- CBD supports recovery
- Doesn't replace fundamentals
- Part of comprehensive approach

## WADA and Testing

### For Competitive Athletes

Important:
- CBD is permitted (WADA)
- THC is prohibited
- Use THC-free products if tested
- Verify with lab reports

### Risk Mitigation

If tested:
- THC-free or isolate only
- Third-party tested
- Document what you use

## My Take

Recovery is where CBD really shines for athletes. The anti-inflammatory properties align perfectly with what muscles need after hard training, and the sleep benefits support the overnight recovery process.

I'd suggest topical CBD for specific sore spots plus oral CBD in the evening for sleep quality. This two-pronged approach addresses both localized soreness and systemic recovery needs.
