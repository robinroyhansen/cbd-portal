## Quick Summary

Golf demands mental focus, physical precision, and often involves walking 18 holes with a repetitive swing motion. CBD may help golfers with focus, anxiety management, and recovery from the unique physical demands of the game.

## Using CBD for Golf

### Pre-Round Protocol

Timing:
- Take 30-60 minutes before
- Low to moderate dose (15-25mg)
- Goal is calm, not sedation
- Maintain alertness

### During the Round

Considerations:
- Effects should last 4-5 hours
- May redose at turn if needed
- Stay hydrated
- Know your response first

### Post-Round Recovery

Approach:
- Higher dose acceptable (25-40mg)
- Topical for specific areas
- Supports recovery
- Evening dose for sleep
