# How to Dispose of CBD Products

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Dispose of CBD products responsibly by mixing oils with an absorbent material before placing in regular rubbish, recycling clean containers, and never pouring oils down drains. Expired or unwanted CBD should be made unusable before disposal. Check local regulations, as some areas have specific guidelines for supplement disposal.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Never pour CBD oil down drains
- Mix with absorbent material before binning
- Recycle clean containers when possible
- Make expired products unusable
- Check local disposal guidelines

---

## Why Proper Disposal Matters

### Environmental Concerns

**Avoid:**
- Oil contaminating water systems
- Unnecessary landfill impact
- Recyclable materials in waste
- Environmental pollution

### Safety Concerns

**Prevent:**
- Children or pets accessing discarded products
- Others using expired products
- Resale of disposed products
- Misuse of containers

---

## Disposing of CBD Oil

### Step-by-Step Method

**1. Mix with Absorbent:**
- Cat litter
- Coffee grounds
- Sawdust
- Flour

**2. Combine in Container:**
- Original container or bag
- Seal securely
- Mark as rubbish

**3. Place in Regular Rubbish:**
- With household waste
- Not recyclable
- Sealed to prevent leaks

### Why Not the Drain

**Problems:**
- Oil clogs pipes
- Environmental contamination
- Water treatment burden
- May be illegal in some areas

---

## Disposing by Product Type

### CBD Oils/Tinctures

**Method:**
1. Empty into absorbent material
2. Mix thoroughly
3. Seal in bag or container
4. Place in rubbish
5. Clean and recycle bottle

### CBD Capsules/Softgels

**Method:**
1. Open capsules and empty if desired
2. Alternatively, crush capsules
3. Mix with absorbent material
4. Seal and dispose in rubbish

### CBD Gummies/Edibles

**Method:**
1. Make unappetising (mix with used coffee grounds)
2. Place in sealed bag
3. Dispose in rubbish
4. Keep away from animals

### CBD Topicals

**Method:**
1. Wipe out remaining product
2. Mix residue with absorbent if significant
3. Dispose in rubbish
4. Recycle container if clean

### CBD Vape Products

**Special Considerations:**
- Check local e-waste guidelines
- Batteries may need special disposal
- Cartridges often not recyclable
- Empty and dispose separately

## Recycling CBD Containers

### Recyclable Items

**Often Recyclable:**
- Glass dropper bottles (clean)
- Some plastic containers
- Cardboard packaging
- Paper inserts

### Preparing for Recycling

**Steps:**
1. Empty completely
2. Remove non-recyclable parts (droppers)
3. Rinse if possible
4. Check local recycling guidelines
5. Place in appropriate bin

### Not Recyclable

**Usually:**
- Dropper assemblies
- Mixed material packaging
- Contaminated containers
- Some plastic types

---

## When to Dispose

### Signs CBD Should Be Disposed

**Definite Disposal:**
- Past expiry date significantly
- Unusual smell or appearance
- Contamination suspected
- Product recall

**Consider Disposal:**
- Approaching expiry
- Product separated and won't remix
- No longer needed
- Unknown storage history

---

## Making Products Unusable

### Why Make Unusable

**Prevents:**
- Someone retrieving and using
- Pet or child consumption
- Resale of expired products
- Misuse

### Methods

**For Oils:**
- Mix with coffee grounds
- Add water and absorbent
- Combine with household waste

**For Edibles:**
- Crush and mix with unappetising material
- Add to used coffee grounds
- Make unappealing

## Pet Safety Considerations

### Keep Away From Pets

**During Disposal:**
- Seal containers
- Don't leave accessible
- Dispose promptly
- Be cautious with edibles

### If Pet Ingests Discarded CBD

- Monitor for symptoms
- Contact vet if concerned
- CBD toxicity in pets is low but possible
- Keep disposal out of reach

## Specific Scenarios

### Expired CBD

**Safe to Dispose:**
- Follow standard methods
- No special handling needed
- May be less potent but not dangerous
- Make unusable before disposing

### Damaged Products

**If Contaminated:**
- Don't use
- Dispose immediately
- Follow standard methods
- Don't try to salvage

### Unwanted Products

**If Unused:**
- Check if friend wants (if fresh)
- Otherwise dispose properly
- Don't sell expired products
- Make unusable before binning

## My Take

Disposing of CBD responsibly takes a few extra minutes but matters for the environment and safety. The key points are simple: never pour oils down drains, mix with absorbent material before binning, and make products unusable to prevent misuse. For containers, recycle what you can. Most CBD disposal is straightforward household waste when handled correctly. If you have large quantities or special circumstances, check with local authorities for specific guidance.
