## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Colombia** with the country having established itself as a major cannabis industry hub. Colombia has favorable regulations for both medical cannabis and consumer CBD products. The market is well-developed with domestic production and exports.

- Growing domestic market

### CBD Products
