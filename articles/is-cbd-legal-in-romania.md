# Is CBD Legal in Romania? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Romania** when derived from EU-approved industrial hemp containing less than 0.2% THC. Romania has a growing CBD market and has become a significant hemp producer in Europe. Products are available in Bucharest and other major cities, with the domestic industry developing.

- Good agricultural climate for hemp

## What's Legal

### Clearly Legal

- CBD oils with <0.2% THC
- CBD cosmetics and topicals
- Hemp food products
- CBD capsules and supplements

### Complex/Restricted

- CBD flowers (enforcement varies)
- High-concentration products
- Products with health claims

### Prohibited

- THC above 0.2%
- Medical claims without authorization
- Sale to minors
