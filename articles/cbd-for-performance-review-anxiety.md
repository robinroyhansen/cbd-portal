# CBD for Performance Review Anxiety

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Performance reviews trigger anxiety even for high performers. The evaluation, potential criticism, and career implications create stress. CBD may help manage review-day anxiety, allowing you to engage more constructively with feedback.

---

## Why Reviews Cause Anxiety

### The Situation

Stressful factors:
- Being formally evaluated
- Potential criticism
- Salary/promotion implications
- Power imbalance
- Past negative experiences

### Common Symptoms

What you might feel:
- Days of dread beforehand
- Sleep disruption
- Physical anxiety symptoms
- Defensive reactions
- Difficulty hearing feedback

---

## How CBD Might Help

### Review Day

Potential benefits:
- Reduced anticipatory anxiety
- Calmer during discussion
- Better able to listen
- Less defensive reactions
- More constructive engagement

### The Week Before

Ongoing anxiety:
- Daily CBD may help
- Reduces rumination
- Better sleep before review
- Clearer thinking about preparation

---

## Using CBD for Reviews

### Timing

Approach:
- Take 45-60 minutes before review
- Allow full effect
- Don't be adjusting during meeting
- Know your response beforehand

### Dosing

For review situations:
- Low-moderate (15-25mg)
- Stay sharp and engaged
- Avoid sedation
- Tested beforehand

### Test First

Essential:
- Don't first-time CBD before review
- Know exactly how it affects you
- Try during less stressful meetings
- Verify you stay sharp

---

## Beyond CBD: Review Preparation

### Before Review

Helpful practices:
- List your accomplishments
- Prepare for likely feedback
- Think about goals
- Have questions ready

### During Review

With reduced anxiety:
- Listen without defensiveness
- Ask clarifying questions
- Take notes
- Engage constructively

### After Review

Processing feedback:
- Separate emotions from content
- Identify actionable items
- Follow up appropriately
- Use feedback productively

---

## When Reviews Are Genuinely Difficult

### Negative Feedback

If expecting criticism:
- CBD helps stay calm
- Listen before reacting
- Ask for specifics
- Request follow-up meeting if needed

### Workplace Issues

Consider:
- If anxiety is severe, address root cause
- Workplace may be genuinely problematic
- CBD doesn't fix bad management
- Know when the issue isn't you

## My Take

Performance reviews can feel high-stakes even when you've done good work. The anxiety often prevents people from engaging constructively with feedback. CBD might help you stay calm enough to actually hear what's being said and respond thoughtfully rather than defensively.

Remember: being less anxious doesn't mean being unprepared. Do your homework on accomplishments and goals, then let CBD help with the emotional side.
