## Quick Summary

Peripheral neuropathy—damage to nerves outside the brain and spinal cord—causes pain, numbness, and weakness. CBD's potential for neuropathic pain makes it worth considering for symptom management.

---

## Understanding Peripheral Neuropathy

### What It Is

Condition:
- Damage to peripheral nerves
- Multiple possible causes
- Affects sensation and movement
- Can be chronic and disabling

### Common Causes

Sources:
- Diabetes (most common)
- Chemotherapy
- Alcohol-related
- Autoimmune conditions
- Vitamin deficiencies
- Idiopathic (unknown)

### Symptoms

Experience:
- Numbness and tingling
- Burning or sharp pain
- Sensitivity to touch
- Muscle weakness
- Balance problems

## Using CBD for Peripheral Neuropathy

### Oral CBD

Primary approach:
- Addresses nerve pain pathway
- Systemic anti-inflammatory
- 30-50mg typical for nerve pain
- Consistent daily use important

### Topical CBD

For affected areas:
- Apply to painful/tingling areas
- May provide local relief
- Feet, hands, legs common areas
- Combine with oral approach

### Combination

Most effective:
- Oral for systemic nerve effects
- Topical for targeted relief
- Addresses multiple aspects
- Comprehensive approach

## Important Considerations

### Finding the Cause

Essential:

- Some causes are reversible
- Proper diagnosis needed

### Standard Treatments

Options:

- Nerve pain medications
