# CBD Return Policies: What to Expect

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD product returns can be tricky due to the nature of consumable products. Understanding typical return policies helps you know what to expect and choose brands that stand behind their products.

---

## Typical CBD Return Policies

### What's Common

Industry standards:
- 30-day return windows common
- Satisfaction guarantees offered by some
- Unopened products easier to return
- Partial refunds for opened products sometimes

### Why It's Complicated

Challenges:
- Consumable product concerns
- Subjective effectiveness
- Regulation variations
- Fraud prevention

---

## Types of Policies

### Satisfaction Guarantees

Best policies:
- Money-back if not satisfied
- Usually 30-60 day window
- May require product return
- Shows company confidence

### Unopened Returns Only

More restrictive:
- Full refund if sealed
- No returns once opened
- Common policy type
- Check before purchasing

### Case-by-Case

Some companies:
- Evaluate each situation
- May offer partial refunds
- Customer service contact required
- Less predictable

---

## Before You Buy

### Check the Policy

Look for:
- Return timeframe
- Condition requirements
- Refund type (full, partial, store credit)
- Who pays return shipping

### Good Signs

Quality brands often:
- Clear, easy-to-find policy
- Reasonable timeframe
- Stand behind products
- Responsive customer service

---

## If You Need to Return

### Steps

Process:
1. Review the policy first
2. Contact customer service
3. Explain the issue
4. Follow their instructions
5. Keep documentation

### Reasons for Returns

Valid reasons:
- Wrong product received
- Damaged in shipping
- Doesn't match description
- Quality issues

### Less Likely Approved

May be denied:
- "Didn't work for me" (subjective)
- Used most of product
- Outside return window
- No receipt/order confirmation

---

## Third-Party Retailers

### Online Marketplaces

Considerations:
- May have their own return policies
- Could differ from brand policy
- Check specific retailer rules
- Amazon, etc. have consumer protections

### Local Stores

In-person purchases:
- Check policy before buying
- Receipt usually required
- Policies vary widely
- No assumption of returns

---

## Avoiding Returns

### Research First

Minimize issues:
- Read reviews thoroughly
- Start with smaller sizes
- Choose reputable brands
- Understand what you're buying

### Manage Expectations

Be realistic:
- CBD isn't magic
- Effects vary by person
- Give it adequate time
- Know what to expect

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Check return policy** before purchasing
2. **Satisfaction guarantees** show brand confidence
3. **Unopened products** easier to return
4. **Keep documentation** of purchases
5. **Contact customer service** politely if issues arise
