# Is CBD Legal in Mexico? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Mexico** when derived from hemp with less than 1% THC. Mexico has moved toward cannabis liberalization, and CBD products are available in stores and online. The market is developing, particularly in major cities like Mexico City.

---

## Mexico's CBD Regulations

### Legal Framework

Mexico's approach:
- CBD from hemp (≤1% THC) is legal
- Higher THC limit than US/EU
- Products available commercially
- Growing market

### Recent Developments

Mexico has liberalized cannabis policy:
- Supreme Court rulings on cannabis rights
- Regulatory framework developing
- CBD market growing
- More progressive than many countries

### Permitted Products

### THC Limit

Mexico's 1% THC limit:
- Higher than US (0.3%) or EU (0.2%)
