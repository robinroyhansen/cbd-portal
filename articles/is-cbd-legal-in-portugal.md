# Is CBD Legal in Portugal? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Portugal** when derived from EU-approved hemp varieties and containing less than 0.2% THC. Portugal's progressive drug policies don't extend to making CBD more available than other EU countries, but the market operates normally within EU frameworks.

---

## Legal Framework

### Portuguese Drug Policy Context

Portugal is famous for drug decriminalization (2001), but this affects personal possession, not CBD regulation:
- Decriminalization applies to personal use quantities of all drugs
- CBD regulation follows EU norms
- Commercial CBD operates within food/supplement rules
- Different from recreational cannabis policy

### CBD Specifically

Under Portuguese law:
- Hemp with <0.2% THC is not a controlled substance
- CBD products follow EU Novel Food regulations
- Cosmetics and topicals permitted
- Food/supplement status being clarified

---

## What's Legal

### Clearly Legal

- CBD from EU-approved hemp (<0.2% THC)
- CBD cosmetics
- CBD topicals for external use
- Hemp foods (seeds, oil)

### Regulated/Complex

- CBD as food supplements (Novel Food applies)
- CBD oils for internal use
- Products sold during Novel Food transition

### Prohibited

- THC above 0.2%
- Medical claims without authorization
- Non-compliant marketing

## Medical Cannabis vs CBD

### Medical Cannabis in Portugal

Portugal has a medical cannabis program:
- Legal since 2018
- Available by prescription
- Pharmacies can dispense
- Growing but limited access

### Over-the-Counter CBD

- Available without prescription
- Cannot make medical claims
- Positioned as wellness products
- More accessible than medical cannabis
