# Is CBD Legal in Pakistan? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Pakistan**. All cannabis products are prohibited under Pakistani law. The country maintains strict prohibition with no distinction between CBD and THC. Do not bring CBD to Pakistan.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Pakistan's stance:
- All cannabis completely prohibited
- Control of Narcotic Substances Act applies
- No medical cannabis program
- No CBD exemptions

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Imprisonment
- Heavy fines
- Criminal record
- Deportation for foreigners
