By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Answer

CBD is in a **legal gray area in Costa Rica**. While hemp-derived CBD isn't explicitly criminalized, the regulatory framework is unclear. Products are available but not formally regulated.

---

## Legal Framework

### Current Status

Costa Rica's situation:
- No clear CBD-specific regulations
- Hemp not explicitly legal or illegal
- Personal use generally tolerated
- Commercial framework developing

### What This Means

Practical reality:
- Products available in some stores
- Quality and legality uncertain
- Not actively prosecuted
- Evolving legal landscape
