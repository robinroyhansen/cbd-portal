# Is CBD Legal in sweden? Complete Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

CBD is legal in sweden when derived from hemp with THC content below the legal limit. As an EU member state, sweden follows the European regulatory framework for CBD products, including Novel Food Regulations.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Hemp-derived CBD is legal in sweden
- THC must be below 0.2-0.3% depending on product type
- Novel Food Regulation applies
- Medical claims on CBD products are prohibited
- Products should have proper testing and labelling

---

## Legal Status

### Current Law

CBD in sweden:
- Legal when hemp-derived
- THC content must comply with EU limits
- Available in shops and online
- Cosmetic and food regulations apply

### Regulations

Products must:
- Come from approved hemp varieties
- Have third-party testing
- Not make medical claims
- Comply with Novel Food requirements

## Travelling with CBD

### Within EU

- Generally permitted
- Keep products in original packaging
- Carry COA documentation

### From Outside EU

- Declare if required
- Ensure product is EU-compliant
