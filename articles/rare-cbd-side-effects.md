# Rare CBD Side Effects: Uncommon Reactions to Know

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

While CBD is generally well-tolerated, rare side effects can occur including liver enzyme changes, mood alterations, and allergic reactions. These uncommon effects typically appear at high doses or in individuals with specific sensitivities or health conditions.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Rare side effects affect a small percentage of users
- Liver enzyme changes possible at very high doses
- Allergic reactions can occur (usually to carrier oils)
- Pre-existing conditions increase risk
- Most rare effects are reversible

---

## Liver-Related Effects

### Elevated Liver Enzymes

- Observed in clinical trials at high doses (1000mg+)
- Usually asymptomatic
- Detected through blood tests
- Generally reversible when CBD stopped

### Who's at Risk

- Those taking hepatotoxic medications
- Pre-existing liver conditions
- Very high dose users
- Combined with certain medications

### Monitoring

- Baseline liver function tests recommended
- Periodic monitoring at high doses
- Watch for jaundice or abdominal pain

---

## Mood and Mental Effects

### Rare Psychological Effects

- Mood changes (rare)
- Irritability (uncommon)
- Agitation (very rare)
- Usually dose-related

### Context

- More common in those with mental health conditions
- May relate to individual biochemistry
- Often resolves with dose adjustment

---

## Allergic Reactions

### Possible Allergens

- **Carrier oils** — Coconut, MCT, hemp seed
- **Terpenes** — Natural plant compounds
- **Additives** — Flavourings, preservatives
- **Hemp plant** — Cross-reactivity with other plants

### Symptoms

- Skin rash or hives
- Itching
- Swelling (rare)
- Respiratory symptoms (very rare)

### Management

- Identify allergen through elimination
- Switch carrier oils
- Try different product types
- Seek medical attention if severe

---

## Hormonal Effects

### Potential Effects

- May affect cortisol levels
- Possible testosterone effects (high doses)
- Research still limited
- Individual variation significant

### Who Should Monitor

- Those with hormonal conditions
- High-dose users
- Long-term users

---

## Cardiovascular Effects

### Rare Reports

- Blood pressure fluctuations
- Heart rate changes
- Usually at higher doses
- Generally mild

### Precautions

- Monitor if you have heart conditions
- Start with low doses
- Consult healthcare provider

---

## When to Seek Medical Help

Contact a healthcare provider immediately if:
- Signs of allergic reaction
- Yellowing of skin or eyes
- Severe mood changes
- Unexplained symptoms
- Symptoms don't resolve

---

## My Take

Rare side effects are exactly that—rare. Most CBD users never experience them. However, awareness is important, especially for high-dose users or those with pre-existing conditions. The key is monitoring your response and consulting healthcare providers when needed.
