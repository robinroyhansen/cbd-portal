## Quick Summary

The endocannabinoid system (ECS) plays a crucial role in maintaining homeostasis—the body's internal balance. CBD interacts with this system in ways that may support the body's natural regulatory processes, helping to maintain equilibrium across various physiological functions.

- Homeostasis means internal balance

## How CBD Supports Homeostasis

### Endocannabinoid Enhancement

**CBD's Effect:**
- Inhibits FAAH enzyme
- Increases anandamide levels
- Prolongs endocannabinoid activity
- Supports natural ECS function

### Receptor Modulation

**Actions:**
- Indirect CB1/CB2 effects
- Activates 5-HT1A (serotonin)
- Modulates TRPV1
- Multiple receptor involvement
