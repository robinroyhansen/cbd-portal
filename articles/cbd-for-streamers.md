# CBD for Streamers: Managing Live Performance Stress

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Live streaming demands sustained energy, audience engagement, and performing under real-time pressure. CBD may help streamers manage pre-stream anxiety, maintain calm during broadcasts, and recover from intensive streaming sessions.

---

## Streaming Challenges

### Live Performance Pressure

What streaming requires:
- Real-time engagement
- Sustained energy
- Audience interaction
- Technical multitasking
- Consistent entertainment

### Streamer Stressors

Common challenges:
- Pre-stream anxiety
- Chat management stress
- Technical difficulties
- Performance pressure
- Schedule demands

---

## How CBD May Help

### Pre-Stream Nerves

Potential benefits:
- Calms pre-broadcast anxiety
- Promotes relaxed state
- Supports natural presence
- Reduces overthinking

### During Stream

Live support:
- Maintains calm under pressure
- Supports audience engagement
- Reduces reactive stress
- Keeps energy balanced

### Post-Stream Recovery

After broadcasting:
- Supports wind-down
- Aids sleep after late streams
- Reduces lingering tension
- Promotes recovery

---

## Timing Strategies

### Before Going Live

Pre-stream approach:
- 30-45 minutes before
- Moderate dose
- Allow settling time
- Avoid sedating amounts

### Extended Streams

For long broadcasts:
- Start with moderate dose
- May redose during breaks
- Keep hydrated
- Monitor energy

### After Stream

Wind-down support:
- Higher dose acceptable
- Support sleep transition
- Reduce overstimulation
- Aid recovery

---

## Dosing Guidelines

### During Streams

Recommended:
- 10-25mg for calming
- Preserve energy and reaction time
- Avoid drowsiness
- Test beforehand

### Post-Stream

Higher doses:
- 25-50mg for recovery
- When alertness isn't needed
- Support sleep after late streams
- Reduce stress accumulation

---

## Practical Considerations

### Energy Management

Balance carefully:
- CBD calms but shouldn't sedate
- Preserve viewer engagement
- Maintain entertainment value
- Find optimal balance

### Testing Protocol

Before relying on CBD:
- Test on practice streams
- Assess effects on performance
- Get feedback if possible
- Adjust accordingly

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Test first** before important streams
2. **Moderate doses** during live performance
3. **Higher doses** acceptable post-stream
4. **Preserve energy** for viewer engagement
5. **Consistent use** helps overall stress
