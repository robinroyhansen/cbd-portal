# Is CBD Legal in Chile? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Chile** for medical use and increasingly available for consumer products. Chile has a established medical cannabis program and CBD products are available through pharmacies and some health stores. The market is relatively accessible compared to other South American countries.

---

## Legal Framework

### Medical Cannabis

Chile's approach:
- Medical cannabis legal
- CBD products available
- Pharmacy distribution
- Prescription pathway

### Consumer Products

Current status:
- Some CBD products available
- Health stores carry CBD
- Growing market
- Relatively accessible

### For Residents
