## Bringing CBD on Flights

### US Domestic Flights

TSA rules:
- Hemp-derived CBD is allowed
- Products must be <0.3% THC
- Keep in original packaging
- Same liquid rules apply

### International Flights

Critical:
- Check destination laws
- Many countries prohibit CBD
- Can't bring if destination bans it
- Research before packing

### What to Bring

Best options:
- Capsules (no liquid rules)
- Gummies (easy, discreet)
- Small oil bottles (3.4oz rule)
- Avoid vapes (often prohibited)

## Using CBD While Flying

### Pre-Flight

Timing:
- Take at airport (after security)
- Or before leaving for airport
- Allow time to work
- Helps with check-in stress

### During Flight

Options:
- Gummies are easiest
- Capsules work well
- Oil if you brought it
- Reapply for long flights

### Post-Flight/Jet Lag

For arrival:
- Evening dose helps sleep adjustment
- Supports new time zone adaptation
- Consistent for several days
- Part of jet lag strategy

## For Different Flight Types

### Short Flights (1-3 hours)

Approach:
- Single dose before flight
- Usually sufficient
- Simple approach

### Long-Haul Flights (6+ hours)

Protocol:
- Dose at departure
- Possible redose mid-flight
- Sleep support important
- Higher total amount

### Red-Eye Flights

Sleep focus:
- Higher dose for sleep
- Combine with sleep strategies
- Neck pillow, eye mask
- Create sleep conditions
