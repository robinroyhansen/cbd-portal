# Is CBD Legal in Luxembourg? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **legal in Luxembourg** when derived from EU-approved hemp with less than 0.3% THC. Luxembourg has become notably progressive on cannabis, with recreational cannabis legalization for personal use. CBD products are widely available in this small but wealthy country.
