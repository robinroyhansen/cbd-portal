# CBD for Runners: Training, Recovery, and Performance

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry

## Quick Summary

Runners increasingly use CBD for recovery, sleep, and managing the aches of high-volume training. CBD's anti-inflammatory properties and sleep benefits align well with runners' needs. This guide covers how runners can incorporate CBD effectively.

---

## Why Runners Use CBD

### Running Demands

Common needs:
- Recovery from high mileage
- Joint and muscle soreness
- Sleep for adaptation
- Inflammation management
- Mental resilience

### How CBD May Help

Potential benefits:
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Sleep improvement
- Pain modulation
- Non-impairing recovery

---

## CBD for Runner Recovery

### Post-Run Application

Approach:
- Oral CBD after long runs
- Topical on sore areas (knees, hips)
- Evening dose for sleep recovery
- Consistent use during heavy training

### Dosing for Runners

Suggestions:
- Daily maintenance: 20-30mg
- Heavy training: 30-50mg
- Post-race recovery: Higher doses
- Individual adjustment needed

## Specific Applications

### For Long Runs

Recovery support:
- Take after completing run
- Anti-inflammatory effects
- Supports overnight recovery
- Part of nutrition routine

### For Sleep

Critical for adaptation:
- Evening CBD dose
- Improves sleep quality
- Better training adaptation
- Rest is performance

### For Aches and Pains

Management:
- Oral for systemic

Caution:

- Know your response

### For Tested Runners

Elite/professional:
- Use THC-free only

### Many Runners Prefer CBD

Reasons:
- May not impair healing
- Safer for regular use
- No GI issues
- Sustainable option
