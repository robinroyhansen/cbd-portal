# CBD for Alcohol Withdrawal: What We Know

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Alcohol withdrawal can be dangerous and requires medical supervision. CBD may help with some aspects of alcohol recovery, but it's not a treatment for acute alcohol withdrawal. Always seek medical help for alcohol withdrawal.

---

## Critical Warning

### Alcohol Withdrawal Can Be Dangerous

Important:
- Withdrawal can cause seizures
- Delirium tremens is life-threatening
- Medical supervision essential
- Don't attempt alone

---

## CBD's Potential Role

### Not for Acute Withdrawal

Clear limitation:
- CBD doesn't treat acute withdrawal
- Medical detox needed
- Benzodiazepines are standard treatment
- CBD is not a substitute

### For Recovery Phase

Where CBD might help:
- Post-detox anxiety
- Sleep disruption in recovery
- Stress management
- Craving reduction (possibly)

---

## Research Status

### Animal Studies

Findings:
- Some neuroprotective effects suggested
- May reduce alcohol-seeking behavior
- Helps anxiety in models
- Theoretical basis exists

### Human Research

Limited:
- Few human trials
- Mostly theoretical currently
- Focus on recovery phase
- Not acute withdrawal

---

## If Considering CBD in Recovery

### Only After Medical Detox

Timing:
- Complete medically supervised detox first
- CBD for recovery support only
- Not during acute phase
- Post-stabilization

### Potential Benefits in Recovery

May help:
- Ongoing anxiety (common in recovery)
- Sleep issues
- Stress that triggers relapse
- Overall wellbeing

### Working with Treatment Team

Essential:
- Inform all providers
- Part of recovery plan
- Monitor progress
- Adjust as needed

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Alcohol withdrawal** needs medical supervision
2. **CBD not for** acute withdrawal
3. **May help** recovery phase
4. **Complete detox first**
5. **Work with** treatment professionals
