# Is CBD Legal in Kuwait? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Kuwait**. All cannabis-derived products are strictly prohibited with severe penalties. Kuwait makes no distinction between CBD and THC. Never bring CBD products to Kuwait.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition

Kuwait's stance:
- Complete cannabis prohibition
- CBD treated as controlled substance
- No medical cannabis program
- Strict enforcement

### Penalties

Consequences:
- Lengthy imprisonment
- Heavy fines
- Deportation for foreigners
- Permanent entry ban
