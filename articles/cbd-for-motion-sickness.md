# CBD for Motion Sickness: Travel Nausea Support

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Summary

Motion sickness affects many travelers, causing nausea, dizziness, and discomfort. CBD's anti-nausea properties may help manage motion sickness symptoms, though research is limited. It offers a natural alternative to traditional motion sickness medications.

---

## Understanding Motion Sickness

### What Causes It

The mechanism:
- Sensory mismatch
- Eyes vs. inner ear conflict
- Brain confusion
- Nausea response

### Common Triggers

Situations:
- Car travel (especially backseat)
- Boats and ships
- Airplanes
- Amusement rides
- VR experiences

### Symptoms

What you experience:
- Nausea
- Cold sweating
- Dizziness
- Pallor
- Vomiting (severe cases)

## How CBD May Help

### Anti-Nausea Effects

Primary benefit:
- CBD has anti-nausea properties
- Well-established for other causes
- May apply to motion sickness
- Endocannabinoid system involvement

### Anxiety Component

Additional support:
- Anxiety can worsen motion sickness
- CBD reduces anxiety
- Breaking the cycle
- Anticipatory nausea help

## Using CBD for Motion Sickness

### Before Travel

Timing:
- Take 1-2 hours before travel
- Allow onset time
- Before symptoms start
- Prevention approach

### Dosing

Recommendations:
- Moderate dose (20-30mg)
- Higher if prone to severe symptoms
- Tested beforehand
- Know your response

### During Travel

If symptoms start:
- Additional dose possible
- Sublingual for faster action
- Combine with other strategies
- Don't wait until severe

## Different Travel Situations

### Car Travel

Approach:
- Take before departure
- Sit in front seat when possible
- Look at horizon
- CBD as support

### Sea Travel

Boat/cruise considerations:
- Start CBD before boarding
- Consistent use throughout
- May need higher doses
- Combine with proven strategies

### Air Travel

Airplane tips:
- Take before flight
- Choose window seat
- Look outside
- CBD for anxiety component too

### Amusement Rides

If prone to sickness:
- CBD before the park
- Strategic ride choices
- Break between intense rides
- Know your limits

## Comparison to Traditional Medications

### Dramamine/Bonine

Traditional options:
- Proven effectiveness
- Often cause drowsiness
- Anticholinergic effects
- Different mechanism

### CBD Alternative

Potential advantages:
- Non-drowsy for most
- Natural option
- No anticholinergic effects
- May combine well

### Using Both

If needed:
- May use CBD alongside
- Reduced medication dose possible
- Discuss with pharmacist
- Individual approach
