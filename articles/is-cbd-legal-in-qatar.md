# Is CBD Legal in Qatar? Complete 2026 Guide

By Robin Roy Krigslund-Hansen | 12+ years in CBD industry
Last updated: January 21, 2026

---

## Quick Answer

CBD is **illegal in Qatar**. The country prohibits all cannabis-derived substances including CBD, with no distinction made for THC content. Penalties are severe. Do not bring CBD to Qatar.

---

## Legal Framework

### Prohibition Status

Qatar's position:
- All cannabis derivatives banned
- CBD classified with narcotics
- No medical cannabis program
- Strict enforcement policy

### Penalties

Potential consequences:
- Imprisonment (up to several years)
- Heavy fines
- Deportation
- Travel ban

---

## For Visitors and Residents

### Clear Warning

Essential:
- Never bring CBD products to Qatar
- THC-free products still illegal
- No medical exemptions
- Airport screening thorough

### What Happens

If caught:
- Immediate detention
- Criminal proceedings
- Likely deportation
- Permanent record
